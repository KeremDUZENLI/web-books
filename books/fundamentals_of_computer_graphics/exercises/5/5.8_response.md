- **Inner Loop (`k`):** Computes a dot product.

- **Interpretations:**

  1. **(i, j, k):** Standard dot product of Row $i$ of A and Column $j$ of B.

  2. **(j, i, k):** Same dot product, just computing column-by-column.

  3. **(i, k, j):** **Row** combination. Each row of A scales a row of B.

  4. **(k, i, j):** **Outer Product** sum. Sum of (Col $k$ of A $\times$ Row $k$ of B).
