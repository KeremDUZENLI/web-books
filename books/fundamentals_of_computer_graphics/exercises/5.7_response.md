- **Parallelepiped:** Defined by three vectors $\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}, \mathbf{c}$, volume is $|\mathbf{a}\mathbf{b}\mathbf{c}|$.

- **Tetrahedron:** A pyramid with a triangular base.

  1. A prism (triangle base) is $1/2$ the volume of the box.

  2. A pyramid is $1/3$ the volume of the prism.

- **Calculation:** $1/2 \times 1/3 = 1/6$.

- **Result:** Volume = $\frac{1}{6} |\mathbf{a}\mathbf{b}\mathbf{c}|$.
