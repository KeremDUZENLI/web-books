- **Matrix:** $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$

- **Decomposition:**

  - The top-left $2 \times 2$ part is $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$. This is a **rotation by $+90^\circ$** (counter-clockwise).

  - The last column is $\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$. This is a **translation by $(1, 1)$**.

- **Result:** The matrix rotates the object 90 degrees counter-clockwise and then moves it by $(1, 1)$ .
