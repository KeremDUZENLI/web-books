- **Application State:** `RectCenter`, `RectCorner`.

- **LMB Down:** `RectCenter = MousePos`.

- **LMB Drag:**

  - `RectCorner = MousePos`.

  - Draw rectangle defined by bounding box $\min(x_c, x_{corner}), \max(x_c, x_{corner})] \times [\min(y_c, y_{corner}), \max(y_c, y_{corner})$. To make it centered, the opposite corner is `Center - (Corner - Center)`.

- **RMB Drag:**

  - `RectCenter = MousePos`.

  - Re-calculate `RectCorner` relative to new center to maintain size.
