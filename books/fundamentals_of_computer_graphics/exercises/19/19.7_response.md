- **Logic:** The "Small View" acts as a camera that pans to follow the `HeroBall`.

- **Update Routine (in Timer Loop):**

  1.  Check if `HeroBall` exists.

  2.  **If yes:**

      - Calculate desired bounds centered on `HeroBall.Center`.

      - `Width = 1.5 * HeroBall.Radius * 2`.

      - `Height = Width` (assuming square aspect ratio).

      - `SmallView.SetWorldBounds(Center - Width/2, Center + Width/2, ...)`.

  3.  **If no:**

      - Disable drawing for Small View or clear to background color.

- **Result:** As the ball moves in the simulation, the coordinate system of the small view shifts every frame, keeping the ball centered.
