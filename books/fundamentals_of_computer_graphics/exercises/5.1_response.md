- **Concept:** Three points $(x, y)$, $(x_0, y_0)$, and $(x_1, y_1)$ lie on the same line if the triangle they form has an area of zero.

- **Formula:**

  $$
  \begin{vmatrix}
  x - x_0 & x_1 - x_0 \\
  y - y_0 & y_1 - y_0
  \end{vmatrix}
  = 0
  $$

- **Expanded:** $(x - x_0)(y_1 - y_0) - (y - y_0)(x_1 - x_0) = 0$.
