- **Goal:** Move a point by $(x_m, y_m, z_m)$.

- **Formula:**

  $$
  \mathbf{T} =
  \begin{bmatrix}
  1 & 0 & 0 & x_m \\
  0 & 1 & 0 & y_m \\
  0 & 0 & 1 & z_m \\
  0 & 0 & 0 & 1
  \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- **Explanation:** In homogeneous coordinates, translation is stored in the fourth column. When multiplied by $(x, y, z, 1)$, the result is $(x+x_m, y+y_m, z+z_m, 1)$.
