- **Goal:** Rotate angle $\theta$ around center $\mathbf{p}(x_p, y_p)$.

- **Steps:**

  1. Move $\mathbf{p}$ to the origin: $\mathbf{T}(-x_p, -y_p)$.

  2. Rotate: $\mathbf{R}(\theta)$.

  3. Move origin back to $\mathbf{p}$: $\mathbf{T}(x_p, y_p)$.

- **Formula:**

  $$
  \mathbf{M} =
  \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & x_p \\ 0 & 1 & y_p \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix} \cos \theta & -\sin \theta & 0 \\ \sin \theta & \cos \theta & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & -x_p \\ 0 & 1 & -y_p \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- **Note:** Standard matrix multiplication order applies (right to left) .
