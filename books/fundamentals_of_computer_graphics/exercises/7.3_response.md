- **Goal:** Find constants $A$ and $B$ for the third row such that $z$ is mapped to $[-1, 1]$.

- **Constraints:**

  - $An + B = -1$ (Near plane maps to -1).

  - $Af + B = 1$ (Far plane maps to +1).

- **Result:**

  - $A = \frac{2}{n-f}$

  - $B = -\frac{n+f}{n-f}$

- **The Third Row:**
  $$[ 0 \quad 0 \quad \frac{2}{n-f} \quad -\frac{n+f}{n-f} ]$$
