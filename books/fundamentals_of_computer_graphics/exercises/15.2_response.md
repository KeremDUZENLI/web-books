- **Polynomial:** $f(u) = a_0 + a_1 u + a_2 u^2$

  - $f'(u) = a_1 + 2 a_2 u$

  - $f''(u) = 2 a_2$

- **Constraints:**

  1.  $f(0) = \mathbf{p}_0 \implies a_0 = p_0$
  2.  $f'(0) = \mathbf{p}_1 \implies a_1 = p_1$
  3.  $f''(0) = \mathbf{p}_2 \implies 2 a_2 = p_2$

- **Constraint Matrix ($C$):**

  $$
  C = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- **Basis Matrix ($B = C^{-1}$):**

  $$
  B = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0.5 \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- **Basis Functions:**

  $$
  b_0(u) = 1, \quad b_1(u) = u, \quad b_2(u) = 0.5 u^2
  $$
