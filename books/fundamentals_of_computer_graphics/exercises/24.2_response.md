- **Goal:** Verify $L = \frac{\Phi}{\pi A}$.

- **Definitions:**

  - $\Phi$: Total outgoing power (flux).

  - $A$: Area.

  - $L$: Radiance (constant for Lambertian).

- **Integration:** Power is the integral of radiance over the area and the hemisphere ($\Omega$).

  $$
  \Phi = \int_A \int_{\Omega} L \cos \theta \, d\omega \, dA
  $$

- **Simplification:**

  - $\int_{\Omega} \cos \theta \, d\omega = \pi$.

  - $\int_A dA = A$.

  - Therefore, $\Phi = L \cdot \pi \cdot A$.

- **Result:** Rearranging gives $L = \frac{\Phi}{\pi A}$.
