- **Goal:** Compute the average of $f(x, y, z) = xyz$ over the unit cube $[0, 1]^3$.

- **Method:** Integrate the function over the volume and divide by the total volume ($V=1$).

  $$
  \text{Avg} = \int_0^1 \int_0^1 \int_0^1 x y z \, dx \, dy \, dz
  $$

- **Calculation:** Since the variables are independent, we can separate the integrals:

  $$
  \left(\int_0^1 x \, dx\right) \left(\int_0^1 y \, dy\right) \left(\int_0^1 z \, dz\right)
  $$

  The integral of $x$ from 0 to 1 is $\frac{1}{2}x^2 \Big|_0^1 = \frac{1}{2}$.

- **Result:** $\frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{8}$.
