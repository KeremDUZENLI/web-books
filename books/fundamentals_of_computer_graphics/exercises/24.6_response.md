- **Goal:** Pick a direction on the hemisphere with uniform probability (not cosine-weighted).

- **PDF:** $p(\omega) = \frac{1}{2\pi}$.

- **Method (Archimedes' Hat-Box Theorem):** The surface area of a sphere slice is proportional to its height ($z$).

- **Mapping:**

  - $\phi = 2\pi \xi_1$ (Azimuth).

  - $z = \xi_2$ (Elevation height, $0$ to $1$).

  - $r = \sqrt{1 - z^2}$ (Radius at height $z$).

- **Vector:**

  $$
  x = \cos(2\pi \xi_1) \sqrt{1 - \xi_2^2}
  $$

  $$
  y = \sin(2\pi \xi_1) \sqrt{1 - \xi_2^2}
  $$

  $$
  z = \xi_2
  $$
