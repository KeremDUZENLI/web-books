- **Goal:** Generate random points $(r, \phi)$ on a disk of radius $R$ without clustering at the center.

- **PDF:** Probability must be proportional to area ($r \, dr$).

- **Mapping:**

  - $\phi = 2\pi \xi_2$ (Uniform angle).

  - $r = R \sqrt{\xi_1}$ (Square root accounts for area increasing with distance from center).

- **Cartesian:**

  - $x = R \sqrt{\xi_1} \cos(2\pi \xi_2)$

  - $y = R \sqrt{\xi_1} \sin(2\pi \xi_2)$
