- **Goal:** Find $\mathbf{M}^{-1}$ for the matrix in Exercise 10.

- **Concept:** This matrix performs the reverse operation: mapping $\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}, \mathbf{c}$ back to $\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w}$.

- **Formula:**

  $$
  \mathbf{M}^{-1} = (\mathbf{B}_{dest} \mathbf{B}_{source}^T)^{-1} = (\mathbf{B}_{source}^T)^{-1} \mathbf{B}_{dest}^{-1} = \mathbf{B}_{source} \mathbf{B}_{dest}^T
  $$

- **Simplified:**

  $$
  \mathbf{M}^{-1} =
  \begin{bmatrix}
  | & | & | & 0 \\
  \mathbf{u} & \mathbf{v} & \mathbf{w} & 0 \\
  | & | & | & 0 \\
  0 & 0 & 0 & 1
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
  - & \mathbf{a} & - & 0 \\
  - & \mathbf{b} & - & 0 \\
  - & \mathbf{c} & - & 0 \\
  0 & 0 & 0 & 1
  \end{bmatrix}
  $$
