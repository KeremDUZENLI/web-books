- **Function:** $f(x, y, z) = x^2 + y - 3z^3$

- **Formula:** $\nabla f = (\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial z})$

- **Derivatives:**

  - $\partial x$: $2x$
  - $\partial y$: $1$
  - $\partial z$: $-9z^2$

- **Result:** $\nabla f = (2x, 1, -9z^2)$.
