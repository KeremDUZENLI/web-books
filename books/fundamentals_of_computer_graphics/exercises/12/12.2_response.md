- **Goal:** Create a hierarchy where moving the "parent" part moves the "child" parts naturally.

- **Diagram Structure:**

  1.  **Root Node:** **Bicycle Frame** (The main coordinate system).

      - **Child 1:** **Rear Wheel** (Transform: Rotate around wheel axis).

      - **Child 2:** **Pedals/Crank** (Transform: Rotate around crank axis).

      - **Child 3:** **Handlebars** (Transform: Rotate around steering column $y$-axis).

        - **Grandchild 3a:** **Front Fork** (Fixed to handlebars).

          - **Great-grandchild 3a-i:** **Front Wheel** (Transform: Rotate around wheel axis).

- **Explanation:** Rotating the Frame moves everything. Rotating the Handlebars turns the Front Fork and Front Wheel (steering). Rotating the Front Wheel spins it (driving) without affecting the steering.
