- **Goal:** Rotate 90 degrees clockwise (which is $-90^\circ$ or $-\pi/2$).

- **Math:** $\cos(-90^\circ) = 0$, $\sin(-90^\circ) = -1$.

- **Formula:**

  $$
  \mathbf{R} =
  \begin{bmatrix}
  0 & 1 \\
  -1 & 0
  \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- **Explanation:** This swaps the axes and negates the new y-coordinate: $(x, y) \to (y, -x)$ .
