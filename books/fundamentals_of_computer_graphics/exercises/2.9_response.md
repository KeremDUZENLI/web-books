- **Goal:** Create basis vectors $\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w}$ from $\mathbf{a}$ and $\mathbf{b}$.

- **Steps:**

  1.  $\mathbf{u} = \frac{\mathbf{a}}{\|\mathbf{a}\|}$ (Normalize $\mathbf{a}$ to get the first vector).

  2.  $\mathbf{w} = \frac{\mathbf{a} \times \mathbf{b}}{\|\mathbf{a} \times \mathbf{b}\|}$ (Cross product gives a vector perpendicular to the plane).

  3.  $\mathbf{v} = \mathbf{w} \times \mathbf{u}$ (Cross product of the new vectors gives the third perpendicular vector).

- **Result:** Basis $\{\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w}\}$ .
