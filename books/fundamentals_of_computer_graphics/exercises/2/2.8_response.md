- **Statement:** $\mathbf{a} \times (\mathbf{b} \times \mathbf{c}) \neq (\mathbf{a} \times \mathbf{b}) \times \mathbf{c}$

- **Counterexample:**
  Let $\mathbf{a} = \mathbf{x} = (1,0,0)$ and $\mathbf{b} = \mathbf{x} = (1,0,0)$ and $\mathbf{c} = \mathbf{y} = (0,1,0)$.

  1.  **Left side:** $\mathbf{x} \times (\mathbf{x} \times \mathbf{y}) = \mathbf{x} \times \mathbf{z} = -\mathbf{y} = (0, -1, 0)$.

  2.  **Right side:** $(\mathbf{x} \times \mathbf{x}) \times \mathbf{y} = \mathbf{0} \times \mathbf{y} = \mathbf{0} = (0,0,0)$.

- **Result:** The results differ, so it is non-associative.
