### Answer 14.5
**Algorithm:**
Let $V[x][y][z]$ be the voxel grid (boolean true/false).
Initialize empty list `faces`.

Iterate $x, y, z$ over grid dimensions:
If $V[x][y][z]$ is true (opaque):
1.  **Check +X neighbor:** If $x+1$ is out of bounds OR $V[x+1][y][z]$ is false:
    * Add Face (+X direction) at position $(x+1, y, z)$.
2.  **Check -X neighbor:** If $x-1$ is out of bounds OR $V[x-1][y][z]$ is false:
    * Add Face (-X direction) at position $(x, y, z)$.
3.  **Check +Y, -Y, +Z, -Z** similarly.

**Result:** Only faces separating an opaque voxel from "air" (or boundary) are generated. Interior faces between two opaque voxels are culled.
