### Answer 14.3
**Algorithm:**
Input: Strip list $S = [v_0, v_1, v_2, \dots, v_n]$.
Output: Triangle list $L = []$.

For $i$ from 0 to $n-2$:
1.  If $i$ is even (0, 2, 4...):
    * Triangle is $(v_i, v_{i+1}, v_{i+2})$.
    * Preserves counter-clockwise winding.
2.  If $i$ is odd (1, 3, 5...):
    * Triangle is $(v_{i+1}, v_i, v_{i+2})$ (or $(v_i, v_{i+2}, v_{i+1})$).
    * Reverses order to maintain consistent winding direction.
3.  Add triangle to $L$.
