### Exercise 28.4
    
For $400<\lambda<700$, consider two monospectral lights, with $Y=1$, one with wavelength $\lambda$ and one with wavelength $\lambda+1$; they are separated by 1 nm in "wavelength space." Plot their distance in XYZ-space as a function of $\lambda$; plot their distance in $L^*u^*v^*$-space as a function of $\lambda$. At what wavelength is this latter difference largest? Smallest? Note: You'll need to find a table of the xy-coordinates of the monospectral points on the CIE horseshoe.
