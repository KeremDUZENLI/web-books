### Exercise 12.4
Enhance the library by defining one-dimensional transformations as well (LinearTransformation1, AffineTransformation1, ProjectiveTransformation1). The first two classes will be almost trivial. The third is more interesting; include a constructor ProjectiveTransform1 (double p, double q, double r) that builds a projective map sending 0 to p, 1 to q, and $\infty$ to r (i.e., $\lim_{x\rightarrow\infty}T(x)=r$). From such a constructor it's easy to build a PointsToPoints transformation.
