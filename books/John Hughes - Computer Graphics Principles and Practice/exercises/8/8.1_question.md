### Exercise 8.1
Suppose you know that triangle (i,j,k) is one of the triangles of a manifold mesh that's represented by a vertex table and a face table. Then edge (i,j) is in the mesh. Describe how to find the other triangle containing edge (i,j). Express the running time of this operation in terms of T, the number of triangles in the mesh. Draw an exemplar class of meshes that shows that the upper bound you found is actually realized in practice.
