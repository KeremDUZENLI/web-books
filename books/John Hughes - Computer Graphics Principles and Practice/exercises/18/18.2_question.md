### Exercise 18.2
    
(An exercise in definitions.) Define $g_{a}(t)=\frac{1}{2a}$ for $|t|<a$ and 0 otherwise.
(a) Show that for any function $f:R\rightarrow R$ the value $U(a)$ defined in Equation 18.26 is $(f*g_{a})(t_{0})$.
(b) Show that the sample of $f$ at $t_{0}$ is $lim_{a\rightarrow0}(f*g_{a})(t_{0})$.
