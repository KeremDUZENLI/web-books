### Answer 10.6
**Process:**
1.  Compute SVD: $M = U D V^T$.
2.  Determine rank $k$ (number of nonzero singular values).
3.  Set the smallest $0.9k$ diagonal entries of $D$ to zero to create $D'$.
4.  Compute compressed image $M' = U D' V^T$.
5.  Comparison: $M'$ appears as a blurred or simplified version of $M$, retaining the most significant structural features (largest singular values) while losing fine detail (noise/texture).
