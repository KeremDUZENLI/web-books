### Answer 10.1
**Algorithm:**
1.  **Define Rectangles:** Let source rectangle $R_1$ have corners $(u_1, v_1)$ and $(u_2, v_2)$, and target rectangle $R_2$ have corners $(x_1, y_1)$ and $(x_2, y_2)$.
2.  **Compute Matrix:** Calculate scale factors $S_x = \frac{x_2-x_1}{u_2-u_1}$ and $S_y = \frac{y_2-y_1}{v_2-v_1}$. Calculate offsets $T_x = x_1 - S_x u_1$ and $T_y = y_1 - S_y v_1$.
    Transformation matrix $M = \begin{bmatrix} S_x & 0 & T_x \\ 0 & S_y & T_y \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
3.  **Process Clicks:** On mouse click at $P_{in} = (u, v)$ inside $R_1$:
    * Draw dot at $(u, v)$.
    * Compute $P_{out} = M [u, v, 1]^T$.
    * Draw dot at $(P_{out}.x, P_{out}.y)$.
4.  **Reset:** Clear dots and rectangles on signal.
