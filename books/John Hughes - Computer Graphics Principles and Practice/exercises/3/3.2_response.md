### Answer 3.2

**Up Vector Changes:**
The Up vector defines the orientation of the camera (which way is "up" on the screen).

* **Original:** $(0, 1, 0)$ (Standard upright).
* **Change to $(1, 0, 0)$:** The camera rotates $90^\circ$. The positive X-axis points "up" on the monitor. The scene appears rotated $90^\circ$ counter-clockwise.
* **Change to $(0, -1, 0)$:** The camera flips upside down ($180^\circ$ rotation). The scene appears inverted.
