### Answer 3.4

**Observation:**
The sphere appears black (or extremely dark).

**Mathematical Reason:**
Lighting is calculated by component-wise multiplication of the light color and the material color.
* **Material (Red):** $(R=1, G=0, B=0)$
* **Light (Blue):** $(R=0, G=0, B=1)$
* **Reflected Light:** $(1\cdot0, 0\cdot0, 0\cdot1) = (0, 0, 0)$ (Black).

**Conclusion:**
A red surface absorbs green and blue light. If illuminated solely by blue light, it reflects nothing.
