### Answer 23.3
    
**Formula:**
A biquadratic Bézier patch uses quadratic Bernstein polynomials:
$B_0^2(t) = (1-t)^2$
$B_1^2(t) = 2t(1-t)$
$B_2^2(t) = t^2$

The surface equation is:
$$ S(u, v) = \sum_{i=0}^{2} \sum_{j=0}^{2} P_{ij} B_i^2(u) B_j^2(v) $$

Expanded terms:
$$
\begin{aligned}
S(u, v) = \quad & P_{00}(1-u)^2(1-v)^2 + P_{01}(1-u)^2 2v(1-v) + P_{02}(1-u)^2 v^2 \\
+ & P_{10}2u(1-u)(1-v)^2 + P_{11}2u(1-u)2v(1-v) + P_{12}2u(1-u)v^2 \\
+ & P_{20}u^2(1-v)^2 + P_{21}u^2 2v(1-v) + P_{22}u^2 v^2
\end{aligned}
$$
