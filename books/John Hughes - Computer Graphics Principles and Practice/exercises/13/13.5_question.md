### Exercise 13.5
Suppose we have a camera with eye at the origin, looking along the z-axis, with up vector the y-axis, and with near -1, far -10, width 4, height 4.
(a) Find a matrix that maps the frustum for this camera to the standard cube $[-1, 1]^3$.
(b) Find a matrix that maps the frustum to the standard cube, but maps the near plane to $z=0$ and the far plane to $z=1$.
