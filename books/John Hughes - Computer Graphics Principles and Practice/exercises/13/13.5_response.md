### Answer 13.5
**Parameters:** $n=1, f=10$, Width $W=4$, Height $H=4$.
Since view is centered, $r=2, l=-2, t=2, b=-2$.
(a) **Map to $[-1, 1]$:**
$M = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2n}{r-l} & 0 & \frac{r+l}{r-l} & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{2n}{t-b} & \frac{t+b}{t-b} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -\frac{f+n}{f-n} & -\frac{2fn}{f-n} \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$
Substitute: $r-l=4, t-b=4, r+l=0, t+b=0$.
$M = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2(1)}{4} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{2(1)}{4} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -\frac{11}{9} & -\frac{20}{9} \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0.5 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0.5 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -1.22 & -2.22 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.

(b) **Map z to $[0, 1]$:**
The $z$ row changes. We need $A(-n) + B = 0$ and $A(-f) + B = w_{far} = f$ (wait, usually maps to homogenous $z/w \in [0,1]$).
After perspective divide: $A + B/z = 0$ at $z=-n$, $A + B/z = 1$ at $z=-f$.
$A - B/n = 0 \implies B = nA$.
$A - B/f = 1 \implies A - nA/f = 1 \implies A(1 - n/f) = 1 \implies A = \frac{f}{f-n}$.
$B = \frac{nf}{f-n}$.
For $z$ row in OpenGL format (where $w_{out} = -z$):
$\frac{-(Az+B)}{z} = -A - B/z$.
So $-A - B/(-n) = 0$ and $-A - B/(-f) = 1$.
Solving this yields $A = -\frac{f}{f-n}$ and $B = -\frac{fn}{f-n}$.
$M_{33} = -\frac{10}{9}, M_{34} = -\frac{10}{9}$.
