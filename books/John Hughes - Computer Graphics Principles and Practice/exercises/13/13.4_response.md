### Answer 13.4
Function: $F(z) = \frac{f+n}{f-n} + \frac{2fn}{f-n}\frac{1}{z}$.
Evaluate at $z = -n$:
$F(-n) = \frac{f+n}{f-n} - \frac{2f}{f-n} = \frac{f+n-2f}{f-n} = \frac{n-f}{f-n} = -1$.
Evaluate at $z = -f$:
$F(-f) = \frac{f+n}{f-n} - \frac{2n}{f-n} = \frac{f+n-2n}{f-n} = \frac{f-n}{f-n} = 1$.
Since $F(z)$ is monotonic for $z < 0$, the interval $[-f, -n]$ maps precisely to $[-1, 1]$.
