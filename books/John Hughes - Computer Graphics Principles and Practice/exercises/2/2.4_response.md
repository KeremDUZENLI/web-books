### Answer 2.4

**Minute Hand (45 minutes):**

- Degrees per minute: $360^{\circ} / 60 = 6^{\circ}$.
- Angle: $45 \times 6^{\circ} = 270^{\circ}$.

**Hour Hand (1:45):**

- Degrees per hour: $360^{\circ} / 12 = 30^{\circ}$.
- Base Angle (1:00): $1 \times 30^{\circ} = 30^{\circ}$.
- Minutes Contribution: 45 minutes is $0.75$ hours.
- Additional Rotation: $0.75 \times 30^{\circ} = 22.5^{\circ}$.
- Total Angle: $30^{\circ} + 22.5^{\circ} = 52.5^{\circ}$.

**Implementation:**

Apply a `RotateTransform` with `Angle="270"` to the minute hand and `Angle="52.5"` to the hour hand.
