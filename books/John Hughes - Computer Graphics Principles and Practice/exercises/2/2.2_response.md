### Answer 2.2

**Unit Conversion:**

- WPF Resolution: 96 units = 1 inch.
- Desired Shift: 1/8 inch.
- Shift in Units: $\frac{1}{8} \times 96 = 12$ units.

**Calculation:**

- Current Translation (centering): $X=48, Y=48$.
- New Translation: $X = 48 + 12 = 60$, $Y = 48 + 12 = 60$.

**Result:**

Update the `TranslateTransform` properties to `X="60"` and `Y="60"`.
