**Observation:**
* **Gouraud Shading:** Colors are interpolated across the faces based on vertex normals. The surface looks smooth and curved in the interior.
* **Silhouette Artifact:** The edges of the object (the silhouette) are determined by the geometry, not the shading. The silhouette remains jagged/polygonal, revealing the low facet count despite the smooth interior shading.
