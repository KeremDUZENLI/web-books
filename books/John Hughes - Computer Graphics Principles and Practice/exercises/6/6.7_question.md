### Exercise 6.7

You might want to visit the "Modeling Curved Surfaces" module of the laboratory to see the effect of changes in the facet count when flat shading is in effect. You can zoom in/out by dragging the mouse (when the cursor is within the viewport) while holding down the right mouse button. Note how increasing the number of facets can only fool the eye at a distance-zooming in exposes the fraud easily. Note also that motion of the object makes the approximation even more obvious, especially at the bottom edge.
