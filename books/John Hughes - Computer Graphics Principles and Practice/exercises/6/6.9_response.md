### Answer 6.9
**Activity:**
Experiment with Ambient, Diffuse, and Specular properties.
**Key Takeaways:**
* **Ambient:** Flattens the image; no depth cues.
* **Diffuse:** Provides shape definition via shading.
* **Specular:** Adds shininess/gloss. Adjusting the exponent ($s$) changes the size of the highlight (High $s$ = small, sharp highlight).
