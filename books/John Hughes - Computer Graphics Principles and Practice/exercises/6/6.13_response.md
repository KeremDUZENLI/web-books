### Answer 6.13
**Problem:**
The Thigh sits at the origin, overlapping the Lower Leg.
**Solution:**
Translate the Thigh upwards to sit on top of the Lower Leg.
**Calculation:**
The Lower Leg assembly puts the knee at roughly $Y=43$.
**Implementation:**
Add `TranslateTransform3D` with `OffsetY="43"` to the Thigh geometry.
