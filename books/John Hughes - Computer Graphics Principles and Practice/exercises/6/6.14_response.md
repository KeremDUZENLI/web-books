### Answer 6.14
**Implementation:**
Add a `RotateTransform3D` to the `LowerLeg` group (not just the shin geometry).
* **Axis:** X-axis `(1, 0, 0)` for a knee bend.
* **Center:** The pivot point of the knee `(0, 50, 0)` relative to the lower leg's coordinate space.
```xml
<RotateTransform3D CenterX="0" CenterY="50" CenterZ="0">
    <RotateTransform3D.Rotation>
        <AxisAngleRotation3D Axis="1 0 0" Angle="37"/>
    </RotateTransform3D.Rotation>
</RotateTransform3D>
```
