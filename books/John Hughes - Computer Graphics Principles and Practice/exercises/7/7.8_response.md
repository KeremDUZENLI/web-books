### Answer 7.8

x-intercept ($y=0$): $4x-6=0 \implies x=1.5$. Point: $(1.5, 0)$.
y-intercept ($x=0$): $2y-6=0 \implies y=3$. Point: $(0, 3)$.
Origin check: $4(0)+2(0)-6 = -6 < 0$. The origin is not in the half-space.
Normal $n=[4, 2]^T$. From origin $(0,0)$, $n$ points to $(4,2)$. $F(4,2) = 16+4-6 = 14 > 0$. The normal points into the positive half-space.
