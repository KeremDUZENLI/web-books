### Exercise 38.1
    
Recall that annual growth at rate r for y years results in $t=r^{y}$ compound growth (see Section 38.2). For example, 1.5x annual growth for five years results in $7.59=1.5^{5}$ compound growth. Derive the formula that gives r as a function of y and t. Using this formula, compute the annual growth rates for ten-year compound growths of 10x, 100x, and 1,000x.
