### Exercise 25.4
    
(a) We sketched an algorithm for computing the boundary of a surface mesh by hashing edges. Adapt this to detect all contours of a mesh, where a contour edge is one where the normals to the two adjacent faces have dot products, with a view vector v, of opposite signs, and the contour is the set of all contour edges.

(b) Contour edges can be generally formed into loops, although as we said, two loops may share one or more vertices. Design an $O(E)$ algorithm for assembling the E contour edges into loops. Hint: Use hashing again.
