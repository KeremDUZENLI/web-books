### Answer 4.2

**Reasoning:**
In a hierarchical scene graph, transformations applied to a parent node propagate to all its children.

1.  The `Torso` is the root node of the robot hierarchy.
2.  The Head, Arms, and Legs were added to the `Children` collection of the Torso (or a group containing the Torso).
3.  Therefore, $M_{final} = M_{parent} \times M_{local}$.
4.  Translating the Torso updates $M_{parent}$, shifting the coordinate space for all sub-components.
