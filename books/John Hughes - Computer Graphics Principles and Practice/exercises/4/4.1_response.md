### Answer 4.1

**Observation:**

- **Torso Y:** Modifies the root translation. Since the Head, Arms, and Legs are children of the Torso node in the scene graph, they inherit this transformation and move in unison.
- **Head Rotation:** Modifies the local rotation of the Head node. This transform is applied only to the Head geometry (and its children, if any), leaving the parent Torso and sibling Arms unaffected.
