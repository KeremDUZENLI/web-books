### Exercise 32.12
    
For a rectangular area light, write code to sample a point from the light uniformly with respect to area. Do the same for a spherical source. For the sphere, recall that the projection $(x,y,z)\mapsto(x/r,y,z/r)$, where $r=\sqrt{x^{2}+z^{2}}$ is an area-preserving map from the unit-radius cylinder about the y-axis, extending from $y=-1$ to $y=1$, onto the unit sphere.
