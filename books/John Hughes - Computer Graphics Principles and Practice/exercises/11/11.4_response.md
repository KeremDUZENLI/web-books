### Answer 11.4
**Reasoning:** If $\theta \approx \pi$, $M$ acts like a reflection through the axis line. Decompose $v = v_{\parallel} + v_{\perp}$.
$M v_{\parallel} = v_{\parallel}$.
$M v_{\perp} \approx -v_{\perp}$.
$v + Mv = (v_{\parallel} + v_{\perp}) + (v_{\parallel} + M v_{\perp}) \approx 2v_{\parallel}$.
The component parallel to the axis is reinforced; the component perpendicular is cancelled.

**Algorithm:**
Iterate $v_{i+1} = \frac{v_i + Mv_i}{||v_i + Mv_i||}$ to isolate the axis vector.
