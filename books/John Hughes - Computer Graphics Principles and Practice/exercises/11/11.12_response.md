### Answer 11.12
1.  Translate $P$ to the origin: $T_{-P}$.
2.  Rotate to align $v$ with the $z$-axis: $R_{align}$.
3.  Rotate by $\theta$ about the $z$-axis: $R_z(\theta)$.
4.  Inverse rotate alignment: $R_{align}^{-1}$.
5.  Translate origin back to $P$: $T_P$.
**Total Transformation:** $M = T_P R_{align}^{-1} R_z(\theta) R_{align} T_{-P}$.
