### Exercise 11.3
Write down the matrices for rotation by $90^{\circ}$ in the xy-plane and rotation by $90^{\circ}$ in the yz-plane. Calling these M and K, verify that $MK\ne KM$ thus establishing that in general, if $R_{1}$ and $R_{2}$ are elements of $SO(3)$, it's not true that $R_{1}R_{2}=R_{2}R_{1}$; this is in sharp contrast to the set $SO(2)$ of $2\times2$ rotation matrices, in which any two rotations commute.
