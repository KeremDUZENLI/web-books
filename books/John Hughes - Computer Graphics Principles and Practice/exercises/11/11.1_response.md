### Answer 11.1
**Rotation formula:** $M = I + \sin(\theta)J_u + (1-\cos\theta)J_u^2$.
**Reflection condition:** $\theta = 180^{\circ} = \pi$.
$\sin(\pi) = 0$, $\cos(\pi) = -1$.
$M = I + 0 \cdot J_u + (1 - (-1))J_u^2 = I + 2J_u^2$.
**Skew-symmetric square:** $J_u^2 = uu^T - (u \cdot u)I = uu^T - I$.
$M = I + 2(uu^T - I) = I + 2uu^T - 2I = 2uu^T - I$.
