- **Concept:** Map geometric properties (shape) to physical properties (physics).

- **Velocity Vector ($\mathbf{v}$):**

  $$
  \mathbf{v} = \mathbf{p}_{end} - \mathbf{p}_{center}
  $$

- **Properties:**

  - **Direction:** The vector points from the center to the mouse release point, establishing the slope.

  - **Speed:** $\|\mathbf{v}\| = \|\mathbf{p}_{end} - \mathbf{p}_{center}\|$. Longer lines (dragging further) result in faster speeds.

- **Usage:** In the simulation timer loop: $\mathbf{p}_{center}^{new} = \mathbf{p}_{center}^{old} + \mathbf{v} \Delta t$.
