- **Goal:** Represent the rotation $\mathbf{R}(-90^\circ)$ as three shears using the Paeth decomposition formula:
  $$\mathbf{R}(\phi) = \mathbf{S}_x(-\tan \frac{\phi}{2}) \mathbf{S}_y(\sin \phi) \mathbf{S}_x(-\tan \frac{\phi}{2})$$

- **Values:** For $\phi = -90^\circ$:

  - $\tan(-45^\circ) = -1$ (so $-\tan = 1$)
  - $\sin(-90^\circ) = -1$

- **Result:**

  $$
  \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ -1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- **Verification:** Multiplying these gives $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
