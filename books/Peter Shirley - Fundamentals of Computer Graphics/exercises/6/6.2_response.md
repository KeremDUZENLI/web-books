- **Goal:** Rotate by angle $\theta$ around the y-axis.

- **Formula:**

  $$
  \mathbf{R}_y(\theta) =
  \begin{bmatrix}
  \cos \theta & 0 & \sin \theta & 0 \\
  0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
  -\sin \theta & 0 & \cos \theta & 0 \\
  0 & 0 & 0 & 1
  \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- **Explanation:** The y-values remain unchanged (row 2 is identity). The x and z values rotate. Note the sign of the sine terms depends on the direction of rotation (right-hand rule).
