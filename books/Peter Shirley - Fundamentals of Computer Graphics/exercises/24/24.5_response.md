- **Method:** Use barycentric coordinates derived from the square root of random numbers (similar to the disk method to avoid clustering at the reference vertex).

- **Mapping:**

  - $\beta = 1 - \sqrt{\xi_1}$

  - $\gamma = \xi_2 \sqrt{\xi_1}$

  - $\alpha = 1 - \beta - \gamma$

- **Point:** $\mathbf{p} = \alpha \mathbf{a} + \beta \mathbf{b} + \gamma \mathbf{c}$
