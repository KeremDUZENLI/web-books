- **Definition:** $V(x) = E[(x - E[x])^2]$. Let $\mu = E[x]$ (which is a constant).

- **Expansion:**

  $$
  V(x) = E[x^2 - 2x\mu + \mu^2]
  $$

- **Linearity of Expectation:**

  $$
  V(x) = E[x^2] - 2\mu E[x] + \mu^2
  $$

- **Substitution:** Since $E[x] = \mu$:

  $$
  V(x) = E[x^2] - 2\mu^2 + \mu^2 = E[x^2] - \mu^2
  $$

- **Result:** $V(x) = E[x^2] - [E(x)]^2$.
