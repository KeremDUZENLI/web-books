- **Context:** Generalization of Exercise 4 to 3D.

- **Formula:** For a 3D convex volume $V$ with Surface Area $S$, the mean chord length is $\frac{4V}{S}$.

- **Values:**

  - Unit Cube Volume $V = 1$.

  - Unit Cube Surface Area $S = 6$ (6 faces of area 1).

- **Calculation:** $\bar{L} = \frac{4(1)}{6}$.

- **Result:** $\frac{2}{3} \approx 0.667$.
