### Answer 1.3.6

**Given:** $P_0(5, 4, -8)$ and normal to plane $3x - 4y + 3z = 7$.

1.  **Find Direction:**
    The line is normal (perpendicular) to the plane. Therefore, the line's direction vector $\mathbf{v}$ is the same as the plane's normal vector $\mathbf{n}$.
    Reading coefficients from the plane equation: $\mathbf{n} = (3, -4, 3)$.

2.  **Parametric Form:**
    $$ x = 5 + 3t $$
    $$ y = 4 - 4t $$
    $$ z = -8 + 3t $$

3.  **Nonparametric Form:**
    $$ \frac{x-5}{3} = \frac{y-4}{-4} = \frac{z+8}{3} $$
