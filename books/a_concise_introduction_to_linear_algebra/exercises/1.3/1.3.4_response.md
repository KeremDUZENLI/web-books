### Answer 1.3.4

**Given:** $P_0(1, -2, 4)$ and $P_1(1, 6, -3)$.

1.  **Direction Vector:**
    $\mathbf{v} = P_1 - P_0 = (0, 8, -7)$.

2.  **Parametric Form:**
    $$ x = 1 $$
    $$ y = -2 + 8t $$
    $$ z = 4 - 7t $$

3.  **Nonparametric Form:**
    Since the $x$-component of the direction is 0, $x$ is constant.
    $$ x = 1, \quad \frac{y+2}{8} = \frac{z-4}{-7} $$
