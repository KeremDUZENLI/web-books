### Answer 1.3.3

**Given:** $P_0(7, -2, 5)$ and $P_1(5, 6, -3)$.

1.  **Find Direction Vector:**
    $\mathbf{v} = P_1 - P_0 = (5-7, 6-(-2), -3-5) = (-2, 8, -8)$.
    We can simplify this direction by dividing by -2 to use $\mathbf{v}' = (1, -4, 4)$ (optional, but cleaner). Let's use the original $\mathbf{v}$ for directness.

2.  **Parametric Form:**
    $$ x = 7 - 2t $$
    $$ y = -2 + 8t $$
    $$ z = 5 - 8t $$

3.  **Nonparametric Form:**
    $$ \frac{x-7}{-2} = \frac{y+2}{8} = \frac{z-5}{-8} $$
