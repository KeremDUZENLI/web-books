### Answer 1.3.15

**Given:** Plane through $P_0(5, 4, -8)$ and **orthogonal** to line $\mathbf{p} = (3, -2, 1) + t(2, 1, -3)$.

1.  **Find Normal:**
    $\mathbf{n} = (2, 1, -3)$ (Line direction).

2.  **Nonparametric Form:**
    $2(x-5) + 1(y-4) - 3(z+8) = 0$.
    $2x + y - 3z - 10 - 4 - 24 = 0$.
    $2x + y - 3z = 38$.

3.  **Parametric Form:**
    Using orthogonal vectors found in 1.3.14:
    $\mathbf{p} = (5, 4, -8) + s(1, -2, 0) + t(0, 3, 1)$.
