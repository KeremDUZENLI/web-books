    Multiplier $l_{21} = 2/1 = 2$.
    $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1$.
    $$ U = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
    $$ L = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$

2.  **Factor out D from U:**
    We want $U_{original} = D U_{new}$ where $U_{new}$ has 1s on diagonal.
    Diagonal entries of $U$ are $1, 0$.
    $$ D = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
    Note: Since the second pivot is 0, the second row is all zeros. We can technically write $U_{new}$ with a 1 in the diagonal, but the row is zeroed by $D$.
    $$ U_{new} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
    (Check: $D U_{new} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$).
