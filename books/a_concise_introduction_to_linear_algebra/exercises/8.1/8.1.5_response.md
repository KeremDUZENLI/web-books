### Answer 8.1.5

**Matrix $m \times n$ (assume $m \le n$ for pivots):**
To compute $LU$:
Step $k$ (for $k=1 \dots m-1$):
* Multipliers: $m-k$ divisions.
* Update submatrix of size $(m-k) \times (n-k)$.
    Ops: $(m-k)(n-k)$ multiplications + subtractions.
    Total $\approx \sum_{k=1}^{m} (m-k)(n-k) = \sum_{j=1}^{m} j(n-m+j)$ (let $j=m-k$).
    $\approx \int_0^m (m-x)(n-x) dx$.
    Or simpler approximation:
    If $m=n$: $n^3/3$.
    If $m < n$: It is roughly $m^2 n - \frac{m^3}{3}$.
