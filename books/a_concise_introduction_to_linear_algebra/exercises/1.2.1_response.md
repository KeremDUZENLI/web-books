###Response 1.2.1###

Let \( \mathbf{p} = (5, 5) \) and \( \mathbf{q} = (1, -7) \).

a. Determine \( \mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q} \) and \( \mathbf{p} - \mathbf{q} \).

b. Represent \( \mathbf{p}, \mathbf{q}, \mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q}, \mathbf{p} - \mathbf{q} \) by arrows in a parallelogram.

c. Compute \( |\mathbf{p}|, |\mathbf{q}|, |\mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q}|, |\mathbf{p} - \mathbf{q}| \).

d. Is \( |\mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q}|^{2} = |\mathbf{p}|^{2} + |\mathbf{q}|^{2} \)?
