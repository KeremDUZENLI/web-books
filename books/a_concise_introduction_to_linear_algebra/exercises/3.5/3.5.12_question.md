### Exercise 3.5.12

Let
$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 & 1 \\
0 & 2 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\quad \text{and} \quad
B = \begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 & -1 \\
0 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 2 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
$$

a. Find a basis for each of $Col(A)$, $Col(B)$, and $Col(A+B)$.

b. Find a basis for each of $Col(A) + Col(B)$ and $Col(A) \cap Col(B)$.

c. Is $Col(A+B) = Col(A) + Col(B)$?

d. Verify the formula $dim(U + V) = dim(U) + dim(V) - dim(U \cap V)$ of Exercise 3.5.23 for $U = Col(A)$ and $V = Col(B)$.
