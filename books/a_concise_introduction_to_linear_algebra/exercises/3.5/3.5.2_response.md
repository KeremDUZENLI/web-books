### Answer 3.5.2

**Matrix:** $A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 & 2 & -1 \end{bmatrix}$.
Rank $r=1$. $n=5, m=1$.

**a. Dimensions:**
$dim(Row)=1, dim(Col)=1, dim(Null)=4, dim(Left\text{-}null)=0$.

**b. Decomposition:**
$\mathbf{x} = (1, 1, 1, 1, 1)^T$.
$Row(A)$ is spanned by $\mathbf{v} = (1, 1, 1, 2, -1)^T$.
$\mathbf{x}_R = \text{proj}_{\mathbf{v}} \mathbf{x} = \frac{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{v}}{\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}} \mathbf{v}$.
$\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{v} = 1(1)+1(1)+1(1)+1(2)+1(-1) = 4$.
$\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v} = 1+1+1+4+1 = 8$.
$\mathbf{x}_R = \frac{4}{8} \mathbf{v} = \frac{1}{2} (1, 1, 1, 2, -1)^T = (0.5, 0.5, 0.5, 1, -0.5)^T$.
$\mathbf{x}_0 = \mathbf{x} - \mathbf{x}_R = (0.5, 0.5, 0.5, 0, 1.5)^T$.
