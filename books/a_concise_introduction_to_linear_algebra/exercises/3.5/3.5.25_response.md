### Answer 3.5.25

**Formula for 3 Subspaces:**
$dim(U+V+W) = dim(U+V) + dim(W) - dim((U+V) \cap W)$.
Substitute $dim(U+V)$:
$dim(U+V+W) = dim(U) + dim(V) - dim(U \cap V) + dim(W) - dim((U+V) \cap W)$.
Note: $(U+V) \cap W \supseteq (U \cap W) + (V \cap W)$, but equality does not always hold (non-distributive lattice).
So the formula is usually left in the iterative form or requires specific conditions.
