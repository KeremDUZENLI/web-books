### Answer 2.2.6

**Augmented Matrix:**
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & b_1 \\ -2 & 3 & -1 & b_2 \\ 3 & -3 & 0 & b_3 \\ 2 & 0 & -2 & b_4 \end{array} \right] $$

**Reduction:**
1.  Clear Col 1:
    $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 + 2R_1 \implies (0, 3, -3 \mid b_2 + 2b_1)$
    $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 3R_1 \implies (0, -3, 3 \mid b_3 - 3b_1)$
    $R_4 \leftarrow R_4 - 2R_1 \implies (0, 0, 0 \mid b_4 - 2b_1)$

    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & b_1 \\ 0 & 3 & -3 & 2b_1 + b_2 \\ 0 & -3 & 3 & -3b_1 + b_3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & -2b_1 + b_4 \end{array} \right] $$

2.  Clear Col 2 (using $R_2$):
    $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + R_2$:
    LHS: $-3+3=0$, $3-3=0$.
    RHS: $(-3b_1 + b_3) + (2b_1 + b_2) = -b_1 + b_2 + b_3$.

    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & b_1 \\ 0 & 3 & -3 & 2b_1 + b_2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & -b_1 + b_2 + b_3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & -2b_1 + b_4 \end{array} \right] $$

**Condition:**
$$ -b_1 + b_2 + b_3 = 0 \quad \text{AND} \quad -2b_1 + b_4 = 0 $$
