### Answer 2.2.3

**Augmented Matrix:**
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & b_1 \\ -2 & 3 & -1 & b_2 \\ -6 & 6 & 0 & b_3 \end{array} \right] $$

**Reduction:**
1.  $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 + 2R_1$:
    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & b_1 \\ 0 & 3 & -3 & b_2 + 2b_1 \\ -6 & 6 & 0 & b_3 \end{array} \right] $$
2.  $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + 6R_1$:
    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & b_1 \\ 0 & 3 & -3 & b_2 + 2b_1 \\ 0 & 6 & -6 & b_3 + 6b_1 \end{array} \right] $$
3.  $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 2R_2$:
    LHS becomes all zeros ($6 - 2(3) = 0$, $-6 - 2(-3) = 0$).
    RHS becomes $(b_3 + 6b_1) - 2(b_2 + 2b_1) = b_3 + 6b_1 - 2b_2 - 4b_1$.
    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & b_1 \\ 0 & 3 & -3 & 2b_1 + b_2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 2b_1 - 2b_2 + b_3 \end{array} \right] $$

**Condition:**
For the system to be consistent, the row $0 = \text{RHS}$ must hold.
$$ 2b_1 - 2b_2 + b_3 = 0 $$
