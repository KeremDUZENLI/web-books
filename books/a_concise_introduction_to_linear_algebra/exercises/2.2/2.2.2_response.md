### Answer 2.2.2

We denote pivots by $p$ ($p \neq 0$) and other entries by $*$. There are 8 forms.

1.  **Rank 3:**
    $$ \begin{bmatrix} p & * & * \\ 0 & p & * \\ 0 & 0 & p \end{bmatrix} $$

2.  **Rank 2:**
    * Pivots in col 1, 2:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} p & * & * \\ 0 & p & * \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
    * Pivots in col 1, 3:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} p & * & * \\ 0 & 0 & p \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
    * Pivots in col 2, 3:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & p & * \\ 0 & 0 & p \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$

3.  **Rank 1:**
    * Pivot in col 1:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} p & * & * \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
    * Pivot in col 2:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & p & * \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
    * Pivot in col 3:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & p \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$

4.  **Rank 0:**
    $$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
