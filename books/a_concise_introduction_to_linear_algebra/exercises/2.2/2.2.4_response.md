### Answer 2.2.4

**Augmented Matrix:**
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & -2 & b_1 \\ 2 & -4 & b_2 \\ -6 & 12 & b_3 \end{array} \right] $$

**Reduction:**
1.  $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1$:
    LHS: $2 - 2(1) = 0$ and $-4 - 2(-2) = 0$.
    RHS: $b_2 - 2b_1$.
2.  $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + 6R_1$:
    LHS: $-6 + 6(1) = 0$ and $12 + 6(-2) = 0$.
    RHS: $b_3 + 6b_1$.

    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & -2 & b_1 \\ 0 & 0 & b_2 - 2b_1 \\ 0 & 0 & b_3 + 6b_1 \end{array} \right] $$

**Condition:**
Both zero rows must be consistent.
$$ b_2 - 2b_1 = 0 \quad \text{AND} \quad b_3 + 6b_1 = 0 $$
