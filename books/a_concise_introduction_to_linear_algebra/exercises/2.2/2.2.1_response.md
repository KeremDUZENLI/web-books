### Answer 2.2.1

We denote pivots by $p$ (where $p \neq 0$) and other entries by $*$.

**Possible forms for $2\times2$ echelon matrices:**

1.  **Rank 2 (2 Pivots):**
    $$ \begin{bmatrix} p & * \\ 0 & p \end{bmatrix} $$

2.  **Rank 1 (1 Pivot):**
    * Pivot in column 1:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} p & * \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
    * Pivot in column 2:
        $$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & p \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$

3.  **Rank 0 (0 Pivots):**
    $$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
