### Answer 1.1.7

**Given:**
$m_1=2, \mathbf{r}_1=(2, -1, 4)$
$m_2=3, \mathbf{r}_2=(1, 5, -6)$
$m_3=5, \mathbf{r}_3=(-2, -5, 4)$

**Total Mass:** $M = 2 + 3 + 5 = 10$.

**Center of Mass:**
$\mathbf{r} = \frac{1}{10} [ 2(2, -1, 4) + 3(1, 5, -6) + 5(-2, -5, 4) ]$
$= \frac{1}{10} [ (4, -2, 8) + (3, 15, -18) + (-10, -25, 20) ]$
$= \frac{1}{10} (4+3-10, -2+15-25, 8-18+20)$
$= \frac{1}{10} (-3, -12, 10)$
$= (-0.3, -1.2, 1.0)$.
