### Answer 6.2.6

**System:**
$x_1 + 3x_2 = -1$
$2x_1 + x_2 = 3$

1.  **Det(A):** $1(1) - 3(2) = -5$.
2.  **Det(A1):** Replace col 1 with $\mathbf{b}$.
    $\begin{vmatrix} -1 & 3 \\ 3 & 1 \end{vmatrix} = -1 - 9 = -10$.
3.  **Det(A2):** Replace col 2 with $\mathbf{b}$.
    $\begin{vmatrix} 1 & -1 \\ 2 & 3 \end{vmatrix} = 3 - (-2) = 5$.

**Solutions:**
$x_1 = \frac{-10}{-5} = 2$.
$x_2 = \frac{5}{-5} = -1$.
$\mathbf{x} = (2, -1)^T$.
