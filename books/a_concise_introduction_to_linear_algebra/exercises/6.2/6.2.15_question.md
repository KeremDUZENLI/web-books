### Exercise 6.2.15

Show that in the plane an equation of the form $y=ax^{2}+bx+c$ for the parabola through the distinct points $(x_{1},y_{1})$, $(x_{2},y_{2})$, $(x_{3},y_{3})$ is equivalent to

$$
\begin{vmatrix}y&x^{2}&x&1\\ y_{1}&x_{1}^{2}&x_{1}&1\\ y_{2}&x_{2}^{2}&x_{2}&1\\ y_{3}&x_{3}^{2}&x_{3}&1\end{vmatrix}=0
$$
