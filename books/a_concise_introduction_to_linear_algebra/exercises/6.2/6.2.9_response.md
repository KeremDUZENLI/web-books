### Answer 6.2.9

**Matrix:** $A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 3 \\ 2 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
$Det(A) = -5$.

**Adjugate:**
Cofactors:
$C_{11} = +1$. $C_{12} = -2$.
$C_{21} = -3$. $C_{22} = +1$.
Cofactor Matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 \\ -3 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
Adjugate (Transpose): $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 \\ -2 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.

**Inverse:**
$A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-5} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 \\ -2 & 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -0.2 & 0.6 \\ 0.4 & -0.2 \end{bmatrix}$.
