### Exercise 6.2.18

Show that the volume of a tetrahedron with vertices $(a_{1},a_{2},a_{3})$, $(b_{1},b_{2},b_{3})$, $(c_{1},c_{2},c_{3})$, $(d_{1},d_{2},d_{3})$ is given by the absolute value of

$$
\frac{1}{6}\begin{vmatrix}a_{1}&a_{2}&a_{3}&1\\ b_{1}&b_{2}&b_{3}&1\\ c_{1}&c_{2}&c_{3}&1\\ d_{1}&d_{2}&d_{3}&1\end{vmatrix}
$$
