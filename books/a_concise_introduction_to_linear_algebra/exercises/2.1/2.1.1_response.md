### Answer 2.1.1

**System:**
1) $2x_1 + 2x_2 - 3x_3 = 0$
2) $x_1 + 5x_2 + 2x_3 = 1$
3) $-4x_1 + 6x_3 = 2$

**Augmented Matrix:**
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 2 & 2 & -3 & 0 \\ 1 & 5 & 2 & 1 \\ -4 & 0 & 6 & 2 \end{array} \right] $$

**Elimination Steps:**
1.  Swap $R_1$ and $R_2$ to put the 1 in the pivot position (optional, but convenient):
    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 & 1 \\ 2 & 2 & -3 & 0 \\ -4 & 0 & 6 & 2 \end{array} \right] $$
2.  Eliminate $x_1$ from $R_2$ ($R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1$):
    $$ 2 - 2(1) = 0, \quad 2 - 2(5) = -8, \quad -3 - 2(2) = -7, \quad 0 - 2(1) = -2 $$
3.  Eliminate $x_1$ from $R_3$ ($R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + 4R_1$):
    $$ -4 + 4(1) = 0, \quad 0 + 4(5) = 20, \quad 6 + 4(2) = 14, \quad 2 + 4(1) = 6 $$
    Current Matrix:
    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & -8 & -7 & -2 \\ 0 & 20 & 14 & 6 \end{array} \right] $$
4.  Eliminate $x_2$ from $R_3$. Multiply $R_2$ by $2.5$ and add to $R_3$, or simpler:
    Scale $R_3$ by $1/2$: $0, 10, 7, 3$.
    Scale $R_2$ by $-1$: $0, 8, 7, 2$.
    Let's stick to standard row ops: $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + \frac{20}{8}R_2 = R_3 + 2.5R_2$.
    $20 + 2.5(-8) = 0$.
    $14 + 2.5(-7) = 14 - 17.5 = -3.5$.
    $6 + 2.5(-2) = 6 - 5 = 1$.
    Matrix:
    $$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & -8 & -7 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & -3.5 & 1 \end{array} \right] $$

**Back Substitution:**
1.  $-3.5x_3 = 1 \implies x_3 = -1/3.5 = -2/7$.
2.  $-8x_2 - 7x_3 = -2 \implies -8x_2 - 7(-2/7) = -2 \implies -8x_2 + 2 = -2 \implies -8x_2 = -4 \implies x_2 = 0.5 = 1/2$.
3.  $x_1 + 5x_2 + 2x_3 = 1 \implies x_1 + 5(1/2) + 2(-2/7) = 1$.
    $x_1 + 2.5 - 4/7 = 1$.
    $x_1 = 1 - 2.5 + 4/7 = -1.5 + 4/7 = -3/2 + 4/7 = (-21 + 8)/14 = -13/14$.

**Solution:**
$$ \mathbf{x} = (-13/14, 1/2, -2/7) $$
