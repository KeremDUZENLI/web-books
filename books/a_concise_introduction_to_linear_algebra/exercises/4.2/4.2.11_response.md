### Answer 4.2.11

**Operator:** $L = XD - DX$.
Basis $\{1, x, \dots, x^n\}$.
Apply to $x^k$:
$D(x^k) = k x^{k-1}$.
$X(k x^{k-1}) = k x^k$.
$XD(x^k) = k x^k$.
$X(x^k) = x^{k+1}$.
$D(x^{k+1}) = (k+1) x^k$.
$DX(x^k) = (k+1) x^k$.
$L(x^k) = k x^k - (k+1) x^k = -x^k$.
**Matrix:**
$L$ maps basis vector $\mathbf{b}_k$ to $-\mathbf{b}_k$.
The matrix is $-I$ (negative identity).
**Properties:**
* **Range:** $\mathcal{P}_n$ (Onto).
* **Kernel:** $\{\mathbf{0}\}$ (1-to-1).
* It is an isomorphism (scaling by -1).
