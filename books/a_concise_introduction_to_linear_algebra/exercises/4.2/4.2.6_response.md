### Answer 4.2.6

**Proof:**
$Range(T) = \{ \mathbf{v} \in V \mid \exists \mathbf{u} \in U, T(\mathbf{u}) = \mathbf{v} \}$.
1.  **Zero:** $T(\mathbf{0}_U) = \mathbf{0}_V$. So $\mathbf{0}_V \in Range(T)$.
2.  **Add:** If $\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2 \in Range$, then $\mathbf{v}_1 = T(\mathbf{u}_1), \mathbf{v}_2 = T(\mathbf{u}_2)$.
    $\mathbf{v}_1 + \mathbf{v}_2 = T(\mathbf{u}_1) + T(\mathbf{u}_2) = T(\mathbf{u}_1+\mathbf{u}_2)$.
    So sum is in Range.
3.  **Scale:** $c\mathbf{v}_1 = cT(\mathbf{u}_1) = T(c\mathbf{u}_1)$. In Range.
