### Answer 1.2.4

Vectors: $\mathbf{p} = (-2, 4)$, $\mathbf{q} = (3, -5)$.

1.  **Dot Product:**
    $\mathbf{p} \cdot \mathbf{q} = (-2)(3) + (4)(-5) = -6 - 20 = -26$

2.  **Magnitudes:**
    $|\mathbf{p}| = \sqrt{(-2)^2 + 4^2} = \sqrt{20}$
    $|\mathbf{q}| = \sqrt{3^2 + (-5)^2} = \sqrt{34}$

3.  **Cosine Formula:**
    $\cos \theta = \frac{\mathbf{p} \cdot \mathbf{q}}{|\mathbf{p}| |\mathbf{q}|} = \frac{-26}{\sqrt{20}\sqrt{34}} = \frac{-26}{\sqrt{680}} \approx -0.997$

4.  **Angle:**
    $\theta = \arccos(-0.997) \approx 175.6^\circ$ (or about $3.06$ radians).
