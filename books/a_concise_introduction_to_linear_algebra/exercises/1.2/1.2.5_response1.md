### Answer 1.2.5

Vectors: $\mathbf{p} = (1, -2, 4)$, $\mathbf{q} = (3, 5, 2)$.

1.  **Dot Product:**
    $\mathbf{p} \cdot \mathbf{q} = (1)(3) + (-2)(5) + (4)(2) = 3 - 10 + 8 = 1$

2.  **Magnitudes:**
    $|\mathbf{p}| = \sqrt{1 + 4 + 16} = \sqrt{21}$
    $|\mathbf{q}| = \sqrt{9 + 25 + 4} = \sqrt{38}$

3.  **Cosine Formula:**
    $\cos \theta = \frac{1}{\sqrt{21}\sqrt{38}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{798}} \approx 0.0354$

4.  **Angle:**
    $\theta = \arccos(0.0354) \approx 88.0^\circ$ (nearly perpendicular).
