### Exercise 6.3.8

Prove that for all vectors $\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}, \mathbf{c}, \mathbf{d}$ of $\mathbb{R}^3$ we have

$(\mathbf{a} \times \mathbf{b}) \times (\mathbf{c} \times \mathbf{d}) = [\operatorname{det}(\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{c}, \mathbf{d})] \mathbf{b} - [\operatorname{det}(\mathbf{b}, \mathbf{c}, \mathbf{d})] \mathbf{a}$

and

$(\mathbf{a} \times \mathbf{b}) \times (\mathbf{c} \times \mathbf{d}) = [\operatorname{det}(\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}, \mathbf{d})] \mathbf{c} - [\operatorname{det}(\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}, \mathbf{c})] \mathbf{d}$.
