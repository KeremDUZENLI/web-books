### Answer 7.4.10

**Proof:**
$|U\mathbf{x}|^2 = (U\mathbf{x})^H (U\mathbf{x}) = \mathbf{x}^H U^H U \mathbf{x}$.
Since $U$ is unitary, $U^H U = I$.
$|U\mathbf{x}|^2 = \mathbf{x}^H I \mathbf{x} = \mathbf{x}^H \mathbf{x} = |\mathbf{x}|^2$.
Taking square roots (lengths are non-negative):
$|U\mathbf{x}| = |\mathbf{x}|$.
