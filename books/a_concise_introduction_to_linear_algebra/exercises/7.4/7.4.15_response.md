### Answer 7.4.15

**Matrix:** $A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.

1.  **Eigenvalues:**
    $(1-\lambda)^2 - (-1) = 0 \implies (1-\lambda)^2 = -1 \implies 1-\lambda = \pm i$.
    $\lambda = 1 \mp i$.

2.  **Eigenvectors:**
    * $\lambda = 1-i$:
        $\begin{bmatrix} i & 1 \\ -1 & i \end{bmatrix} \mathbf{v} = 0 \implies i v_1 + v_2 = 0 \implies v_2 = -i v_1$.
        $\mathbf{s}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -i \end{bmatrix}$.
    * $\lambda = 1+i$:
        $\mathbf{s}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ i \end{bmatrix}$ (Conjugate of $\mathbf{s}_1$).
