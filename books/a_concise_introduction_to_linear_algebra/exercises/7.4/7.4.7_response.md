### Answer 7.4.7

**Proof:**
By definition, $A^H = (\overline{A})^T$.
Then $A^{HH} = (A^H)^H = (\overline{(\overline{A})^T})^T$.
Conjugate of conjugate is original: $\overline{\overline{A}} = A$.
Transpose of transpose is original: $(A^T)^T = A$.
The operations commute: $\overline{A^T} = (\overline{A})^T$.
So $A^{HH} = \overline{(\overline{A}^T)^T} = \overline{\overline{A}} = A$.
