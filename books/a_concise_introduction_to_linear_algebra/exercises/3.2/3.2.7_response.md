### Answer 3.2.7

**Set:** $U=\{x \in \mathbb{R}^3 \mid x_{1}\ge0\}$.

**Decision: No, this is not a subspace.**

**Proof (Counterexample for Scalar Multiplication):**
Let $\mathbf{u} = (1, 0, 0)$. In $U$ because $1 \ge 0$.
Let $c = -1$.
$c\mathbf{u} = (-1, 0, 0)$.
Check condition: $-1 \ge 0$. False.
Not closed under negative scaling.
