### Answer 7.1.7

**Matrix:**
$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & -1 \\ -2 & 3 & -1 \\ -6 & 6 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$

**Characteristic Equation:**
$\det(A-\lambda I) = -\lambda^3 + 4\lambda^2 - 5\lambda + 2 = -(\lambda-1)^2(\lambda-2)$.
Eigenvalues: $\lambda = 1, 2$.

**Eigenvectors:**
* $\lambda=1$: $\mathbf{s}_1 = (1, 1, 0)^T$? Check: $A \mathbf{s}_1 = (1, 1, 0)^T$. Yes. Is there another? Rank$(A-I)$ is 2?
    $(A-I) = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & -1 \\ -2 & 2 & -1 \\ -6 & 6 & -1 \end{bmatrix} \implies x_3=0, x_1=x_2$. One vector.
* $\lambda=2$: $\mathbf{s}_2 = (1, 2, -1)$? Check: $1-(-1)=2$. $-2+6-(-1)=5 \neq 4$.
    Solve $(A-2I)\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{0}$. Result $\mathbf{s}_2 = (1, 3, -1)^T$.
