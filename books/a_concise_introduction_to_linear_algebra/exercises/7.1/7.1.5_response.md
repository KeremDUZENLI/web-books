### Answer 7.1.5

**Matrix:**
$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} $$

1.  **Characteristic Polynomial:**
    Expand along row 2: $(2-\lambda) \begin{vmatrix} 2-\lambda & 1 \\ 1 & 2-\lambda \end{vmatrix}$.
    $(2-\lambda)[(2-\lambda)^2 - 1] = (2-\lambda)(\lambda^2 - 4\lambda + 3) = (2-\lambda)(\lambda-3)(\lambda-1)$.
    Eigenvalues: $\lambda_1 = 1, \lambda_2 = 2, \lambda_3 = 3$.

2.  **Eigenvectors:**
    * $\lambda=1$: $(A-I)\mathbf{x}=0 \implies x_1+x_3=0, x_2=0$. $\mathbf{s}_1 = (1, 0, -1)^T$.
    * $\lambda=2$: $(A-2I)\mathbf{x}=0 \implies x_1=0, x_3=0, x_2 \text{ free}$. $\mathbf{s}_2 = (0, 1, 0)^T$.
    * $\lambda=3$: $(A-3I)\mathbf{x}=0 \implies -x_1+x_3=0, -x_2=0 \implies x_1=x_3$. $\mathbf{s}_3 = (1, 0, 1)^T$.
