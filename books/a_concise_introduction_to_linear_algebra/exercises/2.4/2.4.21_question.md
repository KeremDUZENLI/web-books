### Exercise 2.4.21

Show that if two conformable matrices of any size are partitioned into four submatrices each, so that the products and sums make sense, then:

$$ \begin{bmatrix} A & B \\ C & D \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} E & F \\ G & H \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} AE + BG & AF + BH \\ CE + DG & CF + DH \end{bmatrix} $$
