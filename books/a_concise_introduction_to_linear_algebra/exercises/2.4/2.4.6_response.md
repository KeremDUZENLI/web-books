### Answer 2.4.6

**Matrices:**
$A$ ($1 \times 4$).
$B$ ($3 \times 1$).

**1. Product $AB$:**
Dimensions: $(1 \times 4) \times (3 \times 1)$.
Inner dimensions (4 and 3) do not match.
**$AB$ is not defined.**

**2. Product $BA$:**
Dimensions: $(3 \times 1) \times (1 \times 4) \to (3 \times 4)$.
$$ BA = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & 3 & -4 \end{bmatrix} $$
$$ BA = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & -6 & 9 & -12 \\ 2 & -4 & 6 & -8 \\ 1 & -2 & 3 & -4 \end{bmatrix} $$
