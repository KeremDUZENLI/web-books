### Answer 2.5.13

**Operation:** Multiply Row 1 by $c$.
**Matrix $P$:** Apply operation to $I$.
$$ P = \begin{bmatrix} c & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
**Inverse:** Divide Row 1 by $c$ (multiply by $1/c$).
$$ P^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 1/c & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
