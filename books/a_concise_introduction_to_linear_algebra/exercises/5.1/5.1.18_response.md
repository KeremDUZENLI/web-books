### Answer 5.1.18

**Model:** $z = c_1 x + c_2 y + c_3$.
Matrix $A$ has rows $(x_i, y_i, 1)$.
Normal System $A^T A \mathbf{c} = A^T \mathbf{z}$.
Structure of $A^T A$ ($3 \times 3$):
$$ \begin{bmatrix} \sum x_i^2 & \sum x_i y_i & \sum x_i \\ \sum x_i y_i & \sum y_i^2 & \sum y_i \\ \sum x_i & \sum y_i & m \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} c_1 \\ c_2 \\ c_3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \sum x_i z_i \\ \sum y_i z_i \\ \sum z_i \end{bmatrix} $$
