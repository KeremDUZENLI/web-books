### Answer 5.1.16

**Data:** $(1, -2), (-3, 1), (2, 0), (3, -2), (-5, 3)$.
Model: $y = mx + c$.
Matrix Form $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$ where $\mathbf{x} = [m, c]^T$.
$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 \\ 2 & 1 \\ 3 & 1 \\ -5 & 1 \end{bmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{b} = \begin{bmatrix} -2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} $$

1.  $A^T A = \begin{bmatrix} 1+9+4+9+25 & 1-3+2+3-5 \\ -2 & 5 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 48 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{bmatrix}$.
2.  $A^T \mathbf{b} = \begin{bmatrix} -2-3+0-6-15 \\ -2+1+0-2+3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -26 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
3.  Solve $\begin{bmatrix} 48 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} m \\ c \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -26 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
    From eq 2: $-2m + 5c = 0 \implies 2m = 5c \implies m = 2.5c$.
    Substitute to eq 1: $48(2.5c) - 2c = -26$.
    $120c - 2c = -26 \implies 118c = -26$.
    $c = -26/118 = -13/59 \approx -0.22$.
    $m = 2.5(-13/59) = \frac{5}{2} \frac{-13}{59} = \frac{-65}{118} \approx -0.55$.

**Line:** $y = -0.55x - 0.22$.
