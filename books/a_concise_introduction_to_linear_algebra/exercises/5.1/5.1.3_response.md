### Answer 5.1.3

**Goal:** Matrix $A$ for the $x=y$ plane.

**Solution:**
The condition $x=y$ defines the set of vectors $\{(x, x, z)^T\}$.
We can decompose this as:
$$ \begin{bmatrix} x \\ x \\ z \end{bmatrix} = x \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + z \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
The vectors $\mathbf{u} = (1, 1, 0)^T$ and $\mathbf{v} = (0, 0, 1)^T$ span the subspace and are independent.

$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
