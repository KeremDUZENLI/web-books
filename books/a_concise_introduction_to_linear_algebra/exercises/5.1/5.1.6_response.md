### Answer 5.1.6

**Goal:** Projection matrix $P$ onto x-axis.
The x-axis is spanned by $\mathbf{a} = (1, 0, 0)^T$.
$P = \frac{\mathbf{a}\mathbf{a}^T}{\mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{a}}$.
$\mathbf{a}\mathbf{a}^T = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
$\mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{a} = 1$.

$$ P = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
