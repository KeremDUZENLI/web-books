### Answer 4.3.2

**Mapping:** Rectangle $[1, 4] \times [-2, 2]$ to Unit Square $[0, 1] \times [0, 1]$.
Rectangle Width = $4-1 = 3$. Height = $2 - (-2) = 4$.
Corner = $(1, -2)$.

**Strategy:**
1.  Translate corner $(1, -2)$ to origin: $T(-1, 2)$.
2.  Scale width by $1/3$ and height by $1/4$: $S(1/3, 1/4)$.

**Matrix:**
$$ M = S(1/3, 1/4) T(-1, 2) = \begin{bmatrix} 1/3 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1/4 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
$$ M = \begin{bmatrix} 1/3 & 0 & -1/3 \\ 0 & 1/4 & 1/2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
