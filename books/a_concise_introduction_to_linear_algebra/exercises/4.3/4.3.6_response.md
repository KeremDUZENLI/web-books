### Answer 4.3.6

**Translation in plane $x_4 = 1$:**
Standard homogeneous translation matrix by vector $\mathbf{t} = (t_1, t_2, t_3)$.
$$ T = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & t_1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & t_2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & t_3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
When applied to a vector $\mathbf{x} = (x, y, z, 1)^T$, the result is $(x+t_1, y+t_2, z+t_3, 1)^T$.
