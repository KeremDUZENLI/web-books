### Answer 3.6.22

**a. Transition $S$ ($A \to$ Standard):**
This is simply the matrix $P_A$ itself.
$$ S = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 3 & 4 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$

**b. Compute $\mathbf{x}$:**
Multiply $S$ by the coordinates found in 3.6.21.
Example for $(1,1,1,1)^T_A$:
$S (0.5, -1, 0.5, 3.5)^T = \dots = (1, 1, 1, 1)^T$.
