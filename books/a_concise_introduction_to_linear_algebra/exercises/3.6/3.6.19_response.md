### Answer 3.6.19

**Proof:**
$B = S^{-1} A S$.
$Rank(B) = Rank(S^{-1} A S)$.
Since $S$ is invertible, multiplying by $S$ or $S^{-1}$ does not change the rank of a matrix.
$Rank(XY) = Rank(X)$ if $Y$ is invertible. $Rank(YZ) = Rank(Z)$ if $Y$ is invertible.
Therefore, $Rank(B) = Rank(A)$.
(Also implies Nullity is preserved).
