### Answer 3.6.20

**a. Trace Property:**
$Tr(AB) = \sum_i (AB)_{ii} = \sum_i \sum_k A_{ik} B_{ki} = \sum_k \sum_i B_{ki} A_{ik} = \sum_k (BA)_{kk} = Tr(BA)$.

**b. Similarity:**
$Tr(S^{-1} A S) = Tr( (S^{-1} A) S ) = Tr( S (S^{-1} A) ) = Tr( (S S^{-1}) A ) = Tr( I A ) = Tr(A)$.

**c. Example:**
$A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 4 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \implies Tr(A) = 4$.
$B = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \implies Tr(B) = 5$.
Since traces are not equal, the matrices cannot be similar.
