### Exercise 3.6.21

Let
$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\
3 & 4 & 0 & 1 \\
0 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\
0 & -1 & 0 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
$$
be a basis matrix for $\mathbb{R}^{4}$. Find the coordinate vector $x_{A}$ relative to this basis for each of the $\mathbf{x}$ vectors $\mathbf{e}_{1}$, $\mathbf{e}_{4}$, $(1,1,1,1)^{T}$, and $(1,2,3,4)^{T}$.
