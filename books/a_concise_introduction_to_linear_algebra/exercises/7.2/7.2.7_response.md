### Answer 7.2.7

**Proof:**
Let $B = S^{-1} A S$.
$$ \det(B - \lambda I) = \det(S^{-1} A S - \lambda S^{-1} I S) $$
$$ = \det(S^{-1} (A - \lambda I) S) $$
$$ = \det(S^{-1}) \det(A - \lambda I) \det(S) $$
Since scalars commute and $\det(S^{-1}) \det(S) = 1$:
$$ = \det(A - \lambda I) $$
Thus, similar matrices have the same characteristic polynomial.
