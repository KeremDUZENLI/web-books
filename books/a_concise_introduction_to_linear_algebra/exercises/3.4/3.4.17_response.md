### Answer 3.4.17

**Conjecture on Ranks:**
Let $r = \text{Rank}(A)$.
1.  Rank($[A, A]$) = $r$. (Duplicate cols add no new dimensions).
2.  Rank($[A, A, A]$) = $r$.
3.  Rank($[A; A]$) = $r$. (Duplicate rows add no new dimensions).
4.  Rank($[A, A; A, A]$) = $r$.

**Pattern:**
Repetitive tiling of a matrix $A$ does not increase the rank. The rank remains exactly Rank($A$).

**Proof:**
Columns of $[A, A]$ are just the columns of $A$ repeated. Span is unchanged.
Rows of $[A; A]$ are rows of $A$ repeated. Span is unchanged.
