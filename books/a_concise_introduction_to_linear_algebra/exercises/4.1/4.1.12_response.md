### Answer 4.1.12

**Reflection Line:** $ax + by = 0$. Normal vector $\mathbf{n} = (a, b)^T$.
**Formula:** $Ref(\mathbf{x}) = \mathbf{x} - 2\text{proj}_{\mathbf{n}}\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{x} - 2 \frac{\mathbf{n}\mathbf{n}^T}{\|\mathbf{n}\|^2} \mathbf{x}$.
Matrix $M = I - \frac{2}{a^2+b^2} \begin{bmatrix} a^2 & ab \\ ab & b^2 \end{bmatrix}$.
$$ M = \frac{1}{a^2+b^2} \begin{bmatrix} b^2-a^2 & -2ab \\ -2ab & a^2-b^2 \end{bmatrix} $$
