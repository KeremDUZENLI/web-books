### Answer 4.1.13

**Composition of Reflections:**
Let $L_1$ be angle $\theta_1$, $L_2$ be angle $\theta_2$.
Reflecting across $L_1$ then $L_2$:
$Ref_{\theta}(\phi) = 2\theta - \phi$.
$Ref_{\theta_2}(Ref_{\theta_1}(\phi)) = 2\theta_2 - (2\theta_1 - \phi) = 2(\theta_2 - \theta_1) + \phi$.
This is a rotation by angle $2(\theta_2 - \theta_1)$ (twice the angle between the lines).
