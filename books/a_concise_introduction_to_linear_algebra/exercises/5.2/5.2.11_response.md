### Answer 5.2.11

**Line $L$:** Span of $\mathbf{a} = (1, 3, 5)^T$. $\|\mathbf{a}\|^2 = 1+9+25 = 35$.

**a. Projection Matrices:**
$P_L = \frac{\mathbf{a}\mathbf{a}^T}{35} = \frac{1}{35} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 3 & 9 & 15 \\ 5 & 15 & 25 \end{bmatrix}$.
$P_{L^\perp} = I - P_L = \frac{1}{35} \begin{bmatrix} 34 & -3 & -5 \\ -3 & 26 & -15 \\ -5 & -15 & 10 \end{bmatrix}$.

**b. Sum:**
$P_L + P_{L^\perp} = P_L + (I - P_L) = I$. Checked.

**c. Decomposition of $\mathbf{x} = (2, 3, 4)^T$:**
$\mathbf{x}_L = P_L \mathbf{x}$.
$\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{a} = 2 + 9 + 20 = 31$.
$\mathbf{x}_L = \frac{31}{35} (1, 3, 5)^T$.
$\mathbf{x}_\perp = \mathbf{x} - \mathbf{x}_L = (2, 3, 4) - (31/35, 93/35, 155/35) = (39/35, 12/35, -15/35)$.

**d. Distance:**
Distance $= \|\mathbf{x}_\perp\| = \frac{1}{35} \sqrt{39^2 + 12^2 + (-15)^2} = \frac{1}{35} \sqrt{1521 + 144 + 225} = \frac{\sqrt{1890}}{35} = \frac{3\sqrt{210}}{35} = \frac{3\sqrt{210}}{35} \approx 1.24$.
