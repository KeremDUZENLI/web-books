### Answer 12.1

**1. Define Sphere and Plane Properties**
* Radius $r = 2$ m.
* Initial Center $S = \langle 0, 0, 0 \rangle$ at $t=0$.
* Velocity $V = \langle 2, 0, 1 \rangle$ m/s.
* Plane equation: $x = 10$ (Normal $N = \langle 1, 0, 0 \rangle$, assuming collision from the left).

**2. Parameterize Center Position**
$$P(t) = S + tV = \langle 0, 0, 0 \rangle + t\langle 2, 0, 1 \rangle = \langle 2t, 0, t \rangle$$

**3. Define Collision Condition**
Collision occurs when the distance from the sphere's center to the plane equals the radius $r$.
Since the sphere moves from $x=0$ towards $x=10$, the collision happens at $x_{plane} - r$.
$$x(t) = 10 - 2$$
$$x(t) = 8$$

**4. Solve for $t$**
From the position equation, $x(t) = 2t$.
$$2t = 8$$
$$t = 4 \text{ s}$$
