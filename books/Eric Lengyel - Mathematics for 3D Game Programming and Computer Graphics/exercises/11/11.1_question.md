### Exercise 11.1

Let a cubic curve be defined by the points $P_1$ and $P_2$ and the tangent vectors $T_1$ and $T_2$.
(a) Determine the basis matrix $M_{Hermite}$ corresponding to the geometry vector $G_{Hermite} = \begin{bmatrix} P_1 & P_2 & T_1 & T_2 \end{bmatrix}^T$.
(b) Determine the basis matrix $M_{Bezier}$ corresponding to the geometry vector $G_{Bezier} = \begin{bmatrix} P_1 & P_2 & C_1 & C_2 \end{bmatrix}^T$, where $C_1 = P_1 + T_1/3$ and $C_2 = P_2 - T_2/3$.
