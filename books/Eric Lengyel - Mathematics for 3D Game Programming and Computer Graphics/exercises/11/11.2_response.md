### Answer 11.2

We show that the Cardinal spline basis matrix reduces to the Catmull-Rom basis matrix when the tension parameter $\tau = 1/2$.

**1. Tangent Definitions**
Cardinal splines define tangents at $P_i$ and $P_{i+1}$ as:
$T_i = \tau(1-\tau)(P_{i+1} - P_{i-1}) \xrightarrow{\tau=0.5} 0.5(P_{i+1} - P_{i-1})$
$T_{i+1} = \tau(1-\tau)(P_{i+2} - P_i) \xrightarrow{\tau=0.5} 0.5(P_{i+2} - P_i)$
*Correction: Standard formula is $T_i = \frac{1-t}{2}(P_{i+1} - P_{i-1})$ or simply factor $s = \frac{1-t}{2}$. For Catmull-Rom, factor is 0.5.*

**2. Basis Matrix Construction**
Using the Hermite-to-Geometry mapping for points $P_{i-1}, P_i, P_{i+1}, P_{i+2}$:
$P_1 = P_i$
$P_2 = P_{i+1}$
$T_1 = 0.5 P_{i+1} - 0.5 P_{i-1}$
$T_2 = 0.5 P_{i+2} - 0.5 P_i$

Matrix form $G_{Hermite} = M_{transform} G_{Catmull}$:
$$\begin{bmatrix} P_i \\ P_{i+1} \\ T_1 \\ T_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ -0.5 & 0 & 0.5 & 0 \\ 0 & -0.5 & 0 & 0.5 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} P_{i-1} \\ P_i \\ P_{i+1} \\ P_{i+2} \end{bmatrix}$$

**3. Multiply Matrices**
$M_{CR} = M_{Hermite} \cdot M_{transform}$
$$= \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -2 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 3 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ -0.5 & 0 & 0.5 & 0 \\ 0 & -0.5 & 0 & 0.5 \end{bmatrix}$$

**Row 1 ($t^3$):**
Col 1: $2(0) -2(0) + 1(-0.5) + 1(0) = -0.5$
Col 2: $2(1) -2(0) + 1(0) + 1(-0.5) = 1.5$
Col 3: $2(0) -2(1) + 1(0.5) + 1(0) = -1.5$
Col 4: $2(0) -2(0) + 1(0) + 1(0.5) = 0.5$
Row 1: $[-0.5, 1.5, -1.5, 0.5]$

**Row 2 ($t^2$):**
Col 1: $-3(0) +3(0) -2(-0.5) -1(0) = 1.0$
Col 2: $-3(1) +3(0) -2(0) -1(-0.5) = -2.5$
Col 3: $-3(0) +3(1) -2(0.5) -1(0) = 2.0$
Col 4: $-3(0) +3(0) -2(0) -1(0.5) = -0.5$
Row 2: $[1.0, -2.5, 2.0, -0.5]$

**Row 3 ($t^1$):**
$[-0.5, 0, 0.5, 0]$ (Direct from transform matrix row 3)

**Row 4 ($t^0$):**
$[0, 1, 0, 0]$

**Final Matrix:**
$$M_{Catmull-Rom} = \frac{1}{2} \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 3 & -3 & 1 \\ 2 & -5 & 4 & -1 \\ -1 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$$
This matches Equation (11.46) in the text.
