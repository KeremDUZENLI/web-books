### Answer 5.4

Given:
Horizontal FOV $\alpha_x = 75^\circ$.
Display dimensions $w = 1280$, $h = 1024$.
Aspect ratio $a = \frac{w}{h} = \frac{1280}{1024} = 1.25$.

**Formula:**
The relationship between vertical FOV ($\alpha_y$), horizontal FOV ($\alpha_x$), and aspect ratio ($a$) is:
$$\tan\left(\frac{\alpha_y}{2}\right) = \frac{\tan\left(\frac{\alpha_x}{2}\right)}{a}$$

**Calculation:**
1. $\frac{\alpha_x}{2} = \frac{75^\circ}{2} = 37.5^\circ$.
2. $\tan(37.5^\circ) \approx 0.7673$.
3. $\tan\left(\frac{\alpha_y}{2}\right) = \frac{0.7673}{1.25} \approx 0.6138$.
4. $\frac{\alpha_y}{2} = \arctan(0.6138) \approx 31.54^\circ$.
5. $\alpha_y = 2 \times 31.54^\circ \approx 63.1^\circ$.

Vertical FOV is approximately $63.1^\circ$.
