### Answer 14.7

**1. Force Equation (along incline):**
$$mg \sin \theta - f_s = ma$$
(Gravity pulls down, friction $f_s$ opposes motion).

**2. Torque Equation:**
Torque about center: $\tau = f_s R$.
Moment of inertia for solid sphere: $I = \frac{2}{5}mR^2$.
Rolling condition: $\alpha = a/R$.
$$\tau = I\alpha \implies f_s R = (\frac{2}{5}mR^2)(\frac{a}{R})$$
$$f_s = \frac{2}{5}ma$$

**3. Solve for Acceleration:**
Substitute $f_s$ into the force equation:
$$mg \sin \theta - \frac{2}{5}ma = ma$$
$$mg \sin \theta = ma + \frac{2}{5}ma$$
$$mg \sin \theta = \frac{7}{5}ma$$
$$a = \frac{5}{7}g \sin \theta$$
