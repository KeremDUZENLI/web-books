### Answer 14.8

Toppling occurs when the torque from the applied force exceeds the restoring torque from gravity about the pivot point (the bottom edge opposite the force).

**1. Torques about the pivot:**
* **Applied Force F:** Torque arm is height $z$.
    $\tau_F = Fz$
* **Gravity mg:** Acts at center of mass (horizontal distance $d/2$ from pivot).
    $\tau_g = mg(\frac{d}{2})$

**2. Critical Condition:**
Topple begins when $\tau_F > \tau_g$.
$$Fz > \frac{mgd}{2}$$

**3. Minimum Height z:**
$$z > \frac{mgd}{2F}$$
Thus, the minimum height is $z = \frac{mgd}{2F}$.
