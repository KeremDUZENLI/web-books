### Answer 14.2

**1. Forces acting on the block:**
* **Centrifugal Force:** $F_c = m\omega^2 r$ (pushing outward).
* **Friction Force:** $F_f \le \mu_S N = \mu_S mg$ (resisting motion).

**2. Condition for Sliding:**
Sliding begins when the centrifugal force exceeds the maximum static friction force.
$$F_c > F_{f,max}$$
$$m\omega^2 r > \mu_S mg$$

**3. Solve for $\omega$:**
$$\omega^2 r > \mu_S g$$
$$\omega^2 > \frac{\mu_S g}{r}$$
$$\omega > \sqrt{\frac{\mu_S g}{r}}$$
