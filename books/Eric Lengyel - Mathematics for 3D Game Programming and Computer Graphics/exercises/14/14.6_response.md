### Answer 14.6

**1. Equations of Motion:**
* For the hanging mass $m$: $mg - T = ma$.
* For the spool (mass $M$, radius $R$): Torque $\tau = TR$.
    Angular acceleration $\alpha = a/R$.
    Moment of inertia $I = \frac{1}{2}MR^2$.
    $\tau = I\alpha \implies TR = (\frac{1}{2}MR^2)(\frac{a}{R})$.

**2. Solve for Tension T:**
$$TR = \frac{1}{2}MRa$$
$$T = \frac{1}{2}Ma$$

**3. Solve for Acceleration a:**
Substitute $T$ into the force equation:
$$mg - \frac{1}{2}Ma = ma$$
$$mg = ma + \frac{1}{2}Ma$$
$$mg = a(m + \frac{M}{2})$$
$$a = \frac{mg}{m + M/2} = \frac{2mg}{2m + M}$$
