### Answer 6.8

**1. Snell's Law condition for Critical Angle:**
Total internal reflection occurs when the angle of refraction is $90^\circ$.
$$\eta_1 \sin \theta_c = \eta_2 \sin(90^\circ)$$
$$\eta_1 \sin \theta_c = \eta_2 (1)$$

**2. Values:**
Water (incident medium) $\eta_1 = 1.33$.
Air (transmission medium) $\eta_2 = 1.00$.

**3. Calculation:**
$$1.33 \sin \theta_c = 1.00$$
$$\sin \theta_c = \frac{1}{1.33} \approx 0.7519$$
$$\theta_c = \arcsin(0.7519) \approx 48.75^\circ$$
