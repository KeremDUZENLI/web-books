### Answer 6.2

Function: $f(x) = \ln x + x - 7$.
Derivative: $f'(x) = \frac{1}{x} + 1$.

**Newton's Method Formula:**
$$x_{n+1} = x_n - \frac{f(x_n)}{f'(x_n)} = x_n - \frac{\ln x_n + x_n - 7}{\frac{1}{x_n} + 1}$$

**Iteration (Example starting guess $x_0 = 4$):**
1.  $f(4) = 1.386 + 4 - 7 = -1.614$. $f'(4) = 1.25$.
    $x_1 = 4 - (-1.614 / 1.25) \approx 5.29$.
2.  Repeat until convergence.
    (Root is approximately $x \approx 5.21$).
