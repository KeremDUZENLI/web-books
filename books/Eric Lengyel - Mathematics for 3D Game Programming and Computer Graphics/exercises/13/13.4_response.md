### Answer 13.4

We need the time $t$ when the projectile hits the ground ($z(t)=0$).
$z(t) = z_0 + v_z t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2 = 0$.
$20 + (10\sqrt{2})t - 4.9t^2 = 0$.

**Quadratic Formula:**
$t = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$
$a = -4.9, b = 14.14, c = 20$.
$t = \frac{-14.14 \pm \sqrt{200 - 4(-4.9)(20)}}{-9.8}$
$t = \frac{-14.14 \pm \sqrt{200 + 392}}{-9.8}$
$t = \frac{-14.14 \pm \sqrt{592}}{-9.8}$
$t = \frac{-14.14 \pm 24.33}{-9.8}$

**Solutions:**
$t_1 = \frac{-14.14 - 24.33}{-9.8} \approx 3.92$ s.
$t_2 = \frac{-14.14 + 24.33}{-9.8}$ (Negative time, discard).

**Answer:**
$t \approx 3.92$ seconds.
