### Answer 3.4

The matrix is upper triangular.
$$M = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & 4 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 7 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$

For any upper (or lower) triangular matrix, the eigenvalues are simply the entries on the main diagonal.
The characteristic polynomial is:
$$det(M - \lambda I) = (1-\lambda)(1-\lambda)(1-\lambda)(1-\lambda) = (1-\lambda)^4 = 0$$

**Eigenvalue:**
$\lambda = 1$ (with algebraic multiplicity 4).
