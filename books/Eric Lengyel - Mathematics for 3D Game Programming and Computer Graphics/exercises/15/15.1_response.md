### Answer 15.1

**1. Identify Parameters**
* Frequency $f = 20$ Hz $\implies$ Time step $t = \frac{1}{20} = 0.05$ s.
* Vertex spacing $d = 0.1$ m.
* Damping constant $\mu = 1$ s$^{-1}$.

**2. Stability Formula**
The condition for wave speed $c$ is:
$$c < \frac{d}{2t} \sqrt{\mu t + 2}$$

**3. Calculation**
* Compute inner term: $\mu t + 2 = (1)(0.05) + 2 = 2.05$.
* Compute square root: $\sqrt{2.05} \approx 1.43178$.
* Compute scalar factor: $\frac{d}{2t} = \frac{0.1}{2(0.05)} = \frac{0.1}{0.1} = 1$.
* Maximum speed: $c < 1 \cdot 1.43178$.

**Answer:**
The maximum wave speed is approximately **1.43 m/s**.
