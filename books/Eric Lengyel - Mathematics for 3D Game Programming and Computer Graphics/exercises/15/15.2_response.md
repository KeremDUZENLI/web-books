### Answer 15.2

**1. Identify Parameters**
* Vertex spacing $d = 0.25$ m.
* Wave speed $c = 2$ m/s.
* Damping constant $\mu = 2$ s$^{-1}$.

**2. Stability Formula**
The condition for time step $t$ is:
$$t < \frac{\mu + \sqrt{\mu^2 + 32c^2/d^2}}{8c^2/d^2}$$

**3. Calculation**
* Compute ratio $c/d$: $2 / 0.25 = 8$.
* Compute square $c^2/d^2$: $64$.
* Compute denominator term $8c^2/d^2$: $8(64) = 512$.
* Compute term inside square root: $\mu^2 + 32(64) = 2^2 + 2048 = 4 + 2048 = 2052$.
* Compute square root: $\sqrt{2052} \approx 45.299$.
* Compute numerator: $\mu + 45.299 = 2 + 45.299 = 47.299$.
* Final division: $t < \frac{47.299}{512}$.

$$t < 0.09238$$

**Answer:**
The maximum time step is approximately **0.0924 seconds** (roughly 11 Hz).
