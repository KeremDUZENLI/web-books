### Exercise 8.2.6

a. Show that the condition number $c$ of a symmetric matrix satisfies $c\ge1$.

b. Find all symmetric $2\times2$ matrices with $c=1$.

c. Find the condition number of the matrix $A$ of Equation 8.36 ($[A|\mathbf{b}]=\begin{bmatrix}0.001&1&1\\ 1&1&2\end{bmatrix}$).

d. Find the condition number of the matrix $A$ of Equation 8.40 ($[A|\mathbf{b}]'=\begin{bmatrix}1&1&2\\ 0.001&1&1\end{bmatrix}$).

e. What conclusions can you draw from the answers to Parts a-d?
