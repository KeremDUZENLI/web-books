### Answer 7.2.8

**Proof:**
1.  Assume $A\mathbf{s} = \lambda \mathbf{s}$.
    $$ B (T\mathbf{s}) = (T A T^{-1}) (T \mathbf{s}) = T A (T^{-1} T) \mathbf{s} = T A \mathbf{s} $$
    $$ = T (\lambda \mathbf{s}) = \lambda (T\mathbf{s}) $$
    Thus $T\mathbf{s}$ is an eigenvector of $B$.
2.  The reverse direction follows similarly using $A = T^{-1} B T$.
