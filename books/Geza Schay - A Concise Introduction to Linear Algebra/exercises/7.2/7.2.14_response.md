### Answer 7.2.14

**Predator-Prey:**
$A = \begin{bmatrix} 0.8 & 0.4 \\ r & 2.0 \end{bmatrix}$.
Stable limiting population implies $\lambda_{max} = 1$.
Solve characteristic equation for $r$ such that the largest root is 1.
$$ (0.8-1)(2.0-1) - 0.4r = 0 $$
$$ (-0.2)(1.0) - 0.4r = 0 \implies -0.2 = 0.4r \implies r = -0.5 $$
If $r = -0.5$, $\lambda = 1$ is an eigenvalue.
The eigenvector for $\lambda=1$ gives the stable population ratio.
