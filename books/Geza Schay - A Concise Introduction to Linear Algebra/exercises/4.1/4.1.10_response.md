### Answer 4.1.10

**Given:** $T(\mathbf{a}_i) = \mathbf{b}_i$.
Let $A = [\mathbf{a}_1 \dots \mathbf{a}_n]$ and $B = [\mathbf{b}_1 \dots \mathbf{b}_n]$.
We want matrix $M$ such that $M \mathbf{a}_i = \mathbf{b}_i$ for all $i$.
This is equivalent to $M A = B$.
Since $A$ is a basis matrix, it is invertible.
$M = B A^{-1}$.
$$ [T] = B A^{-1} $$
