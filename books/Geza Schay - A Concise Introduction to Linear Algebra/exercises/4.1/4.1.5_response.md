### Answer 4.1.5

**Transformation:**
$y_1 = 1x_1 - 1x_2$
$y_2 = 2x_1 + 3x_2$
$y_3 = 3x_1 + 2x_2$

**Matrix $[T]$:**
Columns are images of basis vectors $\mathbf{e}_1, \mathbf{e}_2$.
$\mathbf{e}_1 \to (1, 2, 3)^T$.
$\mathbf{e}_2 \to (-1, 3, 2)^T$.
$$ [T] = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 \\ 2 & 3 \\ 3 & 2 \end{bmatrix} $$
