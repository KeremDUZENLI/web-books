### Exercise 4.1.10

Let $A = (\mathbf{a}_1, \mathbf{a}_2, \dots, \mathbf{a}_n)$ be a basis matrix of $\mathbb{R}^n$ and $\mathbf{b}_1, \mathbf{b}_2, \dots, \mathbf{b}_n$ arbitrary vectors of $\mathbb{R}^m$. Let $T$ be the linear transformation from $\mathbb{R}^n$ to $\mathbb{R}^m$ for which $T(\mathbf{a}_i) = \mathbf{b}_i$.

Find the corresponding matrix $[T]$.

(Hint: Use $(T(\mathbf{a}_1), T(\mathbf{a}_2), \dots, T(\mathbf{a}_n)) = [T]A$.)
