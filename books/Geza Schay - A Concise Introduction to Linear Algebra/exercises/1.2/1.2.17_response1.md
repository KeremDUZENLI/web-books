$\mathbf{p} = (3, -4, 12)$.

1.  **Length:** $|\mathbf{p}| = \sqrt{3^2 + (-4)^2 + 12^2} = \sqrt{9+16+144} = \sqrt{169} = 13$.

2.  **Direction Cosines:**
    * $\cos \alpha_1 = 3/13$
    * $\cos \alpha_2 = -4/13$
    * $\cos \alpha_3 = 12/13$

3.  **Angles:**
    * $\alpha_1 = \arccos(3/13) \approx 76.7^\circ$
    * $\alpha_2 = \arccos(-4/13) \approx 107.9^\circ$
    * $\alpha_3 = \arccos(12/13) \approx 22.6^\circ$
