### Answer 7.4.4

**Vectors:** $\mathbf{x}=\begin{bmatrix}2\\ 2i\\ 1+i\end{bmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{y}=\begin{bmatrix}5i\\ 4+i\\ 4-i\end{bmatrix}$.

**a. Conjugates:**
* $\mathbf{x}^H = [2, -2i, 1-i]$.
* $\mathbf{y}^H = [-5i, 4-i, 4+i]$.

**b. Lengths:**
* $|\mathbf{x}|^2 = 4 + 4 + (1+1) = 10 \implies \sqrt{10}$.
* $|\mathbf{y}|^2 = 25 + (16+1) + (16+1) = 59 \implies \sqrt{59}$.

**c. Inner Products:**
* $\mathbf{x}^H \mathbf{y} = 2(5i) + (-2i)(4+i) + (1-i)(4-i) = 10i - 8i + 2 + (4 - i - 4i - 1) = 2i + 2 + 3 - 5i = 5 - 3i$.
* $\mathbf{y}^H \mathbf{x} = 5 + 3i$.
