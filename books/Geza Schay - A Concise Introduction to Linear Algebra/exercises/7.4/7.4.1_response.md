### Answer 7.4.1

**Vectors:** $\mathbf{x}=\begin{bmatrix}2\\ 2i\end{bmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{y}=\begin{bmatrix}5i\\ 4+i\end{bmatrix}$.

**a. Hermitian Conjugates ($\mathbf{x}^H = \overline{\mathbf{x}}^T$):**
* $\mathbf{x}^H = [2, -2i]$.
* $\mathbf{y}^H = [-5i, 4-i]$.

**b. Lengths ($|\mathbf{x}| = \sqrt{\mathbf{x}^H \mathbf{x}}$):**
* $|\mathbf{x}|^2 = 2(2) + (-2i)(2i) = 4 - 4(i^2) = 4 + 4 = 8 \implies |\mathbf{x}| = \sqrt{8} = 2\sqrt{2}$.
* $|\mathbf{y}|^2 = (-5i)(5i) + (4-i)(4+i) = 25 + (16 + 1) = 42 \implies |\mathbf{y}| = \sqrt{42}$.

**c. Inner Products:**
* $\mathbf{x}^H \mathbf{y} = [2, -2i] \begin{bmatrix} 5i \\ 4+i \end{bmatrix} = 10i + (-2i)(4+i) = 10i - 8i - 2i^2 = 2 + 2i$.
* $\mathbf{y}^H \mathbf{x} = [-5i, 4-i] \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2i \end{bmatrix} = -10i + (4-i)(2i) = -10i + 8i - 2i^2 = 2 - 2i$.
    *(Note: $\mathbf{y}^H \mathbf{x} = \overline{\mathbf{x}^H \mathbf{y}}$)*.
