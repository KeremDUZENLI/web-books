### Answer 3.6.12

**Definitions:**
$L_A = P^{-1} L P$, $M_A = P^{-1} M P$ (where $P$ is the change of basis matrix).

**a. Sum:**
$$ (L+M)_A = P^{-1} (L+M) P = P^{-1}LP + P^{-1}MP = L_A + M_A $$

**b. Product:**
$$ (LM)_A = P^{-1} (LM) P = P^{-1} L (P P^{-1}) M P = (P^{-1} L P) (P^{-1} M P) = L_A M_A $$
