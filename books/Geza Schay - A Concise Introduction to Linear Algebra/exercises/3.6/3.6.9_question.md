### Exercise 3.6.9

Let $V$ be the subspace of $\mathbb{R}^{3}$ spanned by the columns of
$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
2 & 4 \\
3 & -1
\end{bmatrix}
\quad \text{and} \quad
B = \begin{bmatrix}
2 & 3 \\
0 & -2 \\
7 & 11
\end{bmatrix}
$$

a. Find the transition matrix from the basis $A$ to the basis $B$.

b. Find the transition matrix from $B$ to $A$.
