### Exercise 6.3.8

- Prove that for all vectors $\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}, \mathbf{c}, \mathbf{d}$ of $\mathbb{R}^{3}$ we have

$$(\mathbf{a}\times \mathbf{b})\times(\mathbf{c}\times \mathbf{d})=[det(\mathbf{a},\mathbf{c},\mathbf{d})]\mathbf{b}-[det(\mathbf{b},\mathbf{c},\mathbf{d})]\mathbf{a}$$

and

$$(\mathbf{a}\times \mathbf{b})\times(\mathbf{c}\times \mathbf{d})=[det(\mathbf{a},\mathbf{b},\mathbf{d})]\mathbf{c}-[det(\mathbf{a},\mathbf{b},\mathbf{c})]\mathbf{d}$$.
