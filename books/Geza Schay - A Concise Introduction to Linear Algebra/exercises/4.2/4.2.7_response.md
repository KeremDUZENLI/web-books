### Answer 4.2.7

**Proof:**
$Ker(T) = \{ \mathbf{u} \in U \mid T(\mathbf{u}) = \mathbf{0}_V \}$.
1.  **Zero:** $T(\mathbf{0}_U) = \mathbf{0}_V$. In Kernel.
2.  **Add:** If $\mathbf{u}_1, \mathbf{u}_2 \in Ker$, then $T(\mathbf{u}_1+\mathbf{u}_2) = T(\mathbf{u}_1) + T(\mathbf{u}_2) = \mathbf{0} + \mathbf{0} = \mathbf{0}$.
3.  **Scale:** $T(c\mathbf{u}_1) = cT(\mathbf{u}_1) = c\mathbf{0} = \mathbf{0}$.
