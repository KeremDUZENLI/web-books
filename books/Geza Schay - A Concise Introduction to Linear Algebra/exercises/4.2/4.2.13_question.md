### Exercise 4.2.13

Let
$$
A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 4 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}
$$
be a basis matrix for a subspace $U$ of $\mathbb{R}^4$. Let
$$
[T] = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 3 & 1 & 0 \\ 4 & 2 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & 3 & 0 & 4 \\ 2 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}
$$
be the matrix of a linear transformation $T$ from $U$ into $\mathbb{R}^6$.

Find the range and kernel of $T$. (Note that $T$ is considered only on $U$ and not on $\mathbb{R}^4$.)
