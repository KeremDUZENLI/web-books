### Answer 4.2.4

**Ex 4.1.5:** $T: \mathbb{R}^2 \to \mathbb{R}^3$. Rank 2.
* **Kernel:** $\{\mathbf{0}\}$. (1-to-1).
* **Range:** Plane spanned by cols of $[T]$. (Not onto).

**Ex 4.1.6:** $T: \mathbb{R}^3 \to \mathbb{R}^3$. $[T] = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
* Sum of rows is 0. Rank < 3. Rank is 2.
* **Kernel:** Line spanned by $(1, 1, 1)^T$. (Not 1-to-1).
* **Range:** Plane $x+y+z=0$ (cols sum to 0). (Not onto).

**Ex 4.1.7:** $T(\mathbf{x}) = (\mathbf{a}^T \mathbf{x})\mathbf{b}$. Rank 1.
* **Kernel:** $\mathbf{a}^\perp$. (Not 1-to-1).
* **Range:** Span$\{\mathbf{b}\}$. (Not onto).

**Ex 4.1.8:** Rank 2 map $\mathbb{R}^2 \to \mathbb{R}^3$.
* **Kernel:** $\{\mathbf{0}\}$. (1-to-1).
* **Range:** Plane. (Not onto).

**Ex 4.1.9:** Rank 2 map $\mathbb{R}^3 \to \mathbb{R}^2$.
* **Kernel:** Dim 1 line. (Not 1-to-1).
* **Range:** $\mathbb{R}^2$. (Onto).
