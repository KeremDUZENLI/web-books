### Answer 2.5.12

**Matrix:** $E = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ c & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
**Effect:**
$EA$ performs: Row 2 of result = $c$(Row 1 of A) + $1$(Row 2 of A).
Other rows unchanged.
This matches the row operation $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 + cR_1$.

**Inverse:**
To undo adding $c$ times $R_1$, we subtract $c$ times $R_1$.
$E^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ -c & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
Check: $E E^{-1} = I$.
