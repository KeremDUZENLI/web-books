### Answer 2.3.1

A reduced echelon matrix (RREF) must satisfy:
1.  Pivots are 1.
2.  Entries above and below pivots are 0.
3.  Zero rows are at the bottom.
4.  Pivots move to the right as you go down.

**Possible $2\times2$ forms:**

1.  **Rank 2 (Identity):**
    $$ \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$

2.  **Rank 1:**
    * Pivot in Col 1: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & k \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$ (where $k$ is any number)
    * Pivot in Col 2: $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$

3.  **Rank 0:**
    $$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
