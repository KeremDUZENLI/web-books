### Answer 2.1.4

**System:** $2x_1 + 2x_2 - 3x_3 = 0$.

**Analysis:**
This is a single equation with 3 unknowns. This represents a plane in $\mathbb{R}^3$ passing through the origin.
There is 1 pivot ($x_1$) and 2 free variables ($x_2, x_3$).

**Parameterization:**
Set $x_2 = s$ and $x_3 = t$.
$2x_1 = -2s + 3t$.
$x_1 = -s + \frac{3}{2}t$.

**Solution:**
$$ \mathbf{x} = s \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + t \begin{bmatrix} 3/2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
