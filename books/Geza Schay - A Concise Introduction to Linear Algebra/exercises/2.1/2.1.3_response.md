### Answer 2.1.3

**System:**
1) $2x_1 + 2x_2 - 3x_3 = 0$
2) $x_1 + 5x_2 + 2x_3 = 1$

**Augmented Matrix:**
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 2 & 2 & -3 & 0 \\ 1 & 5 & 2 & 1 \end{array} \right] $$

**Elimination:**
Swap $R_1, R_2$:
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 & 1 \\ 2 & 2 & -3 & 0 \end{array} \right] $$
$R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1$:
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & -8 & -7 & -2 \end{array} \right] $$

**Solution:**
Variable $x_3$ is free (no pivot). Set $x_3 = t$.
$-8x_2 - 7t = -2 \implies -8x_2 = 7t - 2 \implies x_2 = \frac{1}{4} - \frac{7}{8}t$.
$x_1 + 5x_2 + 2t = 1$.
$x_1 + 5(\frac{1}{4} - \frac{7}{8}t) + 2t = 1$.
$x_1 + \frac{5}{4} - \frac{35}{8}t + \frac{16}{8}t = 1$.
$x_1 = 1 - \frac{5}{4} + \frac{19}{8}t = -\frac{1}{4} + \frac{19}{8}t$.

**Vector Form:**
$$ \mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} -1/4 \\ 1/4 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + t \begin{bmatrix} 19/8 \\ -7/8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
