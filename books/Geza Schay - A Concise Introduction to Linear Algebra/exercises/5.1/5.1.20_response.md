### Answer 5.1.20

**Model:** $y = ax^2 + bx + c$.
Matrix $A$ has rows $(x_i^2, x_i, 1)$. Unknowns $(a, b, c)$.
Normal System $A^T A \mathbf{s} = A^T \mathbf{y}$.

**Coefficients Matrix ($A^T A$):**
$$ \begin{bmatrix} \sum x^4 & \sum x^3 & \sum x^2 \\ \sum x^3 & \sum x^2 & \sum x \\ \sum x^2 & \sum x & m \end{bmatrix} $$

**RHS Vector ($A^T \mathbf{y}$):**
$$ \begin{bmatrix} \sum x^2 y \\ \sum x y \\ \sum y \end{bmatrix} $$
