### Exercise 5.1.21

Let
$$
A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 3 & 4 & 0 \\ 2 & 3 & 2 \\ 0 & -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}
$$

a. Use MATLAB to find the matrix $P$ of the projection onto $Col(A)$.

b. Verify that $P$ is a projection matrix.

c. The command `B = orth(A)` computes a basis matrix for $Col(A)$. Verify that the matrix $P$ projects onto $Col(A)$ by showing that $P\mathbf{e}_i$ is a linear combination of the columns of $B$ for each standard vector $\mathbf{e}_i$ of $\mathbb{R}^4$.
