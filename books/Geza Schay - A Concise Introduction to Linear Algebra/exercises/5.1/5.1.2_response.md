### Answer 5.1.2

**Goal:** Matrix $A$ for the $xy$-plane.

**Solution:**
The $xy$-plane consists of vectors $(x, y, 0)^T$. Dimension is 2.
We need 2 independent columns. The standard basis vectors $\mathbf{e}_1, \mathbf{e}_2$ work perfectly.

$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$
