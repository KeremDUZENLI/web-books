### Answer 3.5.12

**a. Bases:**
* $Col(A)$: Pivot columns of $A$. Rank 2. Basis: $\{(1,0,0)^T, (1,2,0)^T\}$. ($z=0$ plane).
* $Col(B)$: Pivot columns of $B$. Rank 2. Basis: $\{(1,0,2)^T, (-1,0,0)^T\}$. ($y=0$ plane).
* $Col(A+B)$: $A+B = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$. Rank 2. Basis: $\{(1,0,0)^T, (2,2,2)^T\}$.

**b. Sum and Intersection:**
* $Col(A)+Col(B) = Col([A \ B])$. Matrix with columns spanning $z=0$ and $y=0$ planes.
    Includes $(1,0,0)$ and $(0,1,0)$ and $(0,0,1)$? Yes.
    $Col(A)$ contains $(1,0,0)$ and $(0,1,0)$. $Col(B)$ contains $(0,0,1)$ (from combinations).
    Sum is $\mathbb{R}^3$. Basis: Standard basis.
* $Col(A) \cap Col(B)$: Intersection of plane $z=0$ and plane $y=0$.
    This is the $x$-axis. Basis: $\{(1,0,0)^T\}$.

**c. Equality:**
$Col(A+B)$ is a plane. $Col(A)+Col(B)$ is $\mathbb{R}^3$. They are not equal.

**d. Dimension Formula:**
$dim(U+V) = 3$.
$dim(U) + dim(V) - dim(U \cap V) = 2 + 2 - 1 = 3$. Verified.
