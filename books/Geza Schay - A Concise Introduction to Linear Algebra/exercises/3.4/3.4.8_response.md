### Answer 3.4.8

**Proof:**
$AB = A [\mathbf{b}_1 \dots \mathbf{b}_n] = [A\mathbf{b}_1 \dots A\mathbf{b}_n]$.
$AB = O$ if and only if $A\mathbf{b}_i = \mathbf{0}$ for every column $\mathbf{b}_i$ of $B$.
This means every column of $B$ is in $Null(A)$.
Since the columns of $B$ span $Col(B)$, and every basis vector is in $Null(A)$, the entire subspace $Col(B)$ is contained in $Null(A)$.
Thus $Col(B) \subseteq Null(A)$.
