### Exercise 6.2.16

Show that in the plane an equation of the form $x^{2}+y^{2}+ax+by+c=0$ for a circle through the noncollinear points $(x_{1},y_{1})$, $(x_{2},y_{2})$, $(x_{3},y_{3})$ is equivalent to

$$
\begin{vmatrix}x^{2}+y^{2}&x&y&1\\ x_{1}^{2}+y_{1}^{2}&x_{1}&y_{1}&1\\ x_{2}^{2}+y_{2}^{2}&x_{2}&y_{2}&1\\ x_{3}^{2}+y_{3}^{2}&x_{3}&y_{3}&1\end{vmatrix}=0
$$
