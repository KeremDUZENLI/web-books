### Answer 6.2.16

**Equation:**
$$ \begin{vmatrix} x^2+y^2 & x & y & 1 \\ x_1^2+y_1^2 & x_1 & y_1 & 1 \\ \dots & \dots & \dots & 1 \end{vmatrix} = 0 $$
1.  **Form:** Expand row 1.
    $(x^2+y^2) C_1 + x C_2 + y C_3 + C_4 = 0$.
    $x^2 + y^2 + \frac{C_2}{C_1} x + \frac{C_3}{C_1} y + \frac{C_4}{C_1} = 0$.
    This is the general equation of a circle.
2.  **Points:**
    Rows equal if $(x,y)$ matches any data point.
    Satisfies all three points.
