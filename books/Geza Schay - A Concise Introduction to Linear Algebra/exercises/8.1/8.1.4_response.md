### Answer 8.1.4

**Parameters:**
$n = 1000$.
Speed $= 10^5$ ops/sec.
Operations count $\approx \frac{n^3}{3}$.

**Calculation:**
Ops $\approx \frac{(1000)^3}{3} = \frac{10^9}{3} \approx 3.33 \times 10^8$.
Time $= \frac{3.33 \times 10^8}{10^5} = 3.33 \times 10^3$ seconds.
$3333$ seconds $\approx 55.5$ minutes.
