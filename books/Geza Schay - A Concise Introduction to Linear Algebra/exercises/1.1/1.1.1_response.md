### Answer 1.1.1

**a. Free Vectors:**
* $\vec{PR} = \mathbf{r} - \mathbf{p} = (\mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q}) - \mathbf{p} = \mathbf{q}$.
* $\vec{PQ} = \mathbf{q} - \mathbf{p}$.
* $\vec{QP} = \mathbf{p} - \mathbf{q} = -(\mathbf{q} - \mathbf{p})$.

**b. Center Vectors ($C$ is the midpoint of diagonals):**
The diagonals are $\vec{OR}$ and $\vec{PQ}$. The midpoint of $\vec{OR}$ is $\frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{p}+\mathbf{q})$.
* $\vec{OC} = \frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q})$.
* $\vec{PC} = \vec{OC} - \mathbf{p} = \frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q}) - \mathbf{p} = \frac{1}{2}\mathbf{q} - \frac{1}{2}\mathbf{p} = \frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{q} - \mathbf{p})$.
* $\vec{QC} = \vec{OC} - \mathbf{q} = \frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{p} + \mathbf{q}) - \mathbf{q} = \frac{1}{2}\mathbf{p} - \frac{1}{2}\mathbf{q} = \frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{p} - \mathbf{q})$.
