### Answer 7.1.4

**Matrix:**
$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$

1.  **Characteristic Equation:** $\lambda^2 = 0 \implies \lambda = 0$ (multiplicity 2).
2.  **Eigenvectors:**
    $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} = \mathbf{0} \implies x_1 = 0$.
    $x_2$ is free.
    $\mathbf{s} = (0, 1)^T$.
    (There is only one independent eigenvector; the matrix is defective).
