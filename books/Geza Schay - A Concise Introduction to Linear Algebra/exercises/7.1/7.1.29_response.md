### Answer 7.1.29

**Conjecture for Hadamard matrices $H_n$:**
1.  $|\lambda| = \sqrt{n}$. (Since $H$ is scaled orthogonal, vectors length scaled by $\sqrt{n}$).
2.  Eigenvalues are real (if $H$ is symmetric) and equal to $\pm \sqrt{n}$.

**Proof:**
$H \mathbf{x} = \lambda \mathbf{x}$.
$\|H \mathbf{x}\|^2 = \|\lambda \mathbf{x}\|^2 = \lambda^2 \|\mathbf{x}\|^2$.
Also $\|H \mathbf{x}\|^2 = \mathbf{x}^T H^T H \mathbf{x} = \mathbf{x}^T (nI) \mathbf{x} = n \|\mathbf{x}\|^2$.
So $\lambda^2 = n \implies \lambda = \pm \sqrt{n}$.
