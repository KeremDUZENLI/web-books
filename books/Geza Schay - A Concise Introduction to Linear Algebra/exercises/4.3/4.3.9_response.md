### Answer 4.3.9

**Plane:** $x + 2y = 0$. Normal $\mathbf{n} = (1, 2, 0)$.
**Basis for Plane:**
Need vectors orthogonal to $\mathbf{n}$.
$\mathbf{v}_1 = (0, 0, 1)$ (z-axis is in the plane).
$\mathbf{v}_2 = (-2, 1, 0)$ (orthogonal to $\mathbf{n}$ in xy plane).
Orthonormal Basis:
$\mathbf{e}_1 = (0, 0, 1)$.
$\mathbf{e}_2 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} (-2, 1, 0)$.

**Projection/View:**
For each vertex $\mathbf{p}$ of the house:
1.  Project onto plane (optional, or just find coords directly if $\mathbf{p}$ is not on plane).
    View implies orthogonal projection.
    $\mathbf{p}_{proj} = \mathbf{p} - (\mathbf{p} \cdot \mathbf{n}_{unit}) \mathbf{n}_{unit}$.
2.  Coordinates $(r, s)$:
    $r = \mathbf{p} \cdot \mathbf{e}_1 = p_z$.
    $s = \mathbf{p} \cdot \mathbf{e}_2 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} (-2p_x + p_y)$.

**Plotting:**
Calculate $(r, s)$ for all house vertices and connect them.
