### Answer 8.3.4

**Operation:** `A^8 ./ A^7` (Element-wise division).
Let $A$ be diagonalizable with dominant eigenvalue $\lambda_1$.
$A^k \approx c_1 \lambda_1^k \mathbf{u}_1 \mathbf{v}_1^T$ (Power iteration applied to matrix powers).
So $A^8 \approx \lambda_1 A^7$.
The element-wise ratio $(A^8)_{ij} / (A^7)_{ij}$ should approximate $\lambda_1$ for all entries where $(A^7)_{ij}$ is not too small.
**Result for Hilbert(3):**
The matrix `hilb(3)` has a dominant eigenvalue.
The output matrix will have entries all close to the dominant eigenvalue $\lambda_{max} \approx 1.408$.
