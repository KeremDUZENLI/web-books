### Answer 3.3.4

**Goal:** Solve $x_1\mathbf{a}_1 + x_2\mathbf{a}_2 + x_3\mathbf{a}_3 = \mathbf{b}$.
$$ \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 4 & 4 & 1 & 7 \\ 2 & -3 & 3 & 16 \\ 1 & 2 & -1 & -3 \end{array} \right] $$

**Reduction:**
1.  Swap $R_1, R_3$: $(1, 2, -1 \mid -3)$.
2.  $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1$: $(0, -7, 5 \mid 22)$.
3.  $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 4R_1$: $(0, -4, 5 \mid 19)$.
4.  $R_3 \leftarrow 7R_3 - 4R_2$:
    $7(0, -4, 5 \mid 19) - 4(0, -7, 5 \mid 22)$.
    LHS: $(-28+28, 35-20) = (0, 15)$.
    RHS: $133 - 88 = 45$.
    $15x_3 = 45 \implies x_3 = 3$.
5.  $-7x_2 + 5(3) = 22 \implies -7x_2 = 7 \implies x_2 = -1$.
6.  $x_1 + 2(-1) - 3 = -3 \implies x_1 = 2$.

**Result:**
$\mathbf{b} = 2\mathbf{a}_1 - \mathbf{a}_2 + 3\mathbf{a}_3$.
