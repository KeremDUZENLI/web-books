### Exercise 5.2.8

1. Find an orthonormal basis for the subspace $U$ of $\mathbb{R}^4$ spanned by the vectors $\mathbf{a}_1 = (0, 0, -1, 1)^T$, $\mathbf{a}_2 = (1, 0, 0, 1)^T$, and $\mathbf{a}_3 = (1, 0, -1, 0)^T$.

2. Extend the orthonormal basis above to an orthonormal basis for $\mathbb{R}^4$.

3. Find the $QR$ factorization of the matrix $A = (\mathbf{a}_1, \mathbf{a}_2, \mathbf{a}_3)$.
