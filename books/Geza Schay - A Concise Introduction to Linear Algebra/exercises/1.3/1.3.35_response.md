### Answer 1.3.35

**Problem:** $P_0(3, 4, 0)$ to $y - 2z = 5$.

1.  **Normalize:**
    $\mathbf{v} = (0, 1, -2)$. Magnitude $\sqrt{5}$.
    $\mathbf{n} = (0, 1, -2)/\sqrt{5}$.
    $d = 5/\sqrt{5} = \sqrt{5}$.

2.  **Apply Function:**
    $f(P_0) = \frac{0(3) + 1(4) - 2(0)}{\sqrt{5}} - \sqrt{5}$.
    $f(P_0) = \frac{4}{\sqrt{5}} - \frac{5}{\sqrt{5}} = \frac{-1}{\sqrt{5}}$.

3.  **Distance:**
    $1/\sqrt{5} \approx 0.45$. (Matches 1.3.27).
