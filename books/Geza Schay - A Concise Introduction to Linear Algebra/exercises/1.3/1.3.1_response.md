### Answer 1.3.1

**Given:** Point $P_0(1, -2, 4)$ and direction $\mathbf{v} = (2, 3, -5)$.

1.  **Parametric Form:**
    The vector equation is $\mathbf{p} = \mathbf{p}_0 + t\mathbf{v}$.
    $$ (x, y, z) = (1, -2, 4) + t(2, 3, -5) $$
    Breaking into components:
    $$ x = 1 + 2t $$
    $$ y = -2 + 3t $$
    $$ z = 4 - 5t $$

2.  **Nonparametric Form (Symmetric Equations):**
    Solve each parametric equation for $t$ and equate them.
    $$ t = \frac{x-1}{2} = \frac{y+2}{3} = \frac{z-4}{-5} $$
