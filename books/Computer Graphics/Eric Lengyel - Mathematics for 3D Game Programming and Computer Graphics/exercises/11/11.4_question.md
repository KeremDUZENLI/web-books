### Exercise 11.4

Let $Q(u)$ be a nonuniform B-spline lying in the x-y plane having control points $P_0 = \langle 0,0 \rangle$, $P_1 = \langle 1,2 \rangle$, $P_2 = \langle 2,2 \rangle$, and $P_3 = \langle 3,0 \rangle$. Suppose the knot vector is $\{0,0,0,0,1,1,1,1\}$. Use Böhm subdivision to insert a new knot at $t' = \frac{1}{2}$ and determine the new control points $P_0'$ through $P_4'$.
