### Exercise 32.2
    
(a) Let $A=(0,0)$, $B=(1,0)$, and $C=(1,1)$, and let T be the triangle ABC. Suppose that the texture coordinates for A are $(u_{A},v_{A})=(0.2, 0.6)$, for B they're (0.3, 0.3), and for C they're (0.5, 0.1). Assume that texture coordinates are interpolated linearly across the triangle. Find the unit vector u such that the u-coordinate increases fastest in direction u.
(b) Generalize to arbitrary point and texture coordinates at the three vertices.
