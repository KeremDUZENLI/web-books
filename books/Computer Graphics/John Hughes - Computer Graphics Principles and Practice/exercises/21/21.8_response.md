### Answer 21.8
    
**Reordering Approaches:**
1.  **Z-axis Mapping:** Map the "pinch" gesture not just to scale, but to Z-height. Lifting fingers "pulls" the photo off the table (larger drop shadow). Dropping it places it on top.
2.  **Long Press:** A long press (dwell) on a photo triggers a mode change where the photo "pops" to the front layer visually.
3.  **Shuffle Gesture:** A distinct gesture, like a three-finger swipe or a "shake" of the photo, sends it to the back.
4.  **Menu/Widget:** Tap to select, then tap a "Bring to Front" icon in a contextual menu (less fluid, but unambiguous).
