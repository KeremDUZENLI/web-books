### Exercise 9.4
Show that the interpolation of values across a triangle using the scanline method is the same as the one defined using the barycentric method. Hint: Show that if the triangle is in the xy-plane, then each of the methods defines a function of the form $f(x,y)=Ax+By+C$. Now suppose you have two such functions with the same values at the three vertices of a triangle. Explain why they must take the same values at all interior points as well.
