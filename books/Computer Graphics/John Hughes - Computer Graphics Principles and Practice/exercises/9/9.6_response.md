### Answer 9.6
**Problems Observed:**
* **Minification/Aliasing:** When mapping the triangle to a large area in the texture (zooming out), individual pixels in the display triangle skip over many pixels in the texture. This causes aliasing (Moiré patterns) where the checkerboard turns into noise.
* **Magnification/Blurring:** When mapping to a tiny texture area (zooming in), large blocks of screen pixels map to single texture pixels, resulting in blocky or blurry large squares.
* **Anisotropy:** Mapping to a tall, narrow triangle distorts the aspect ratio, causing the checks to stretch into long rectangles.
