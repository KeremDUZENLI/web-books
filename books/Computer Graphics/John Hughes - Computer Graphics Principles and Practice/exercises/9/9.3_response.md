### Answer 9.3
For a nonboundary vertex in a solid mesh composed of tetrahedra:
* **Star:** The star is the union of all tetrahedra (and their sub-simplices) meeting at the vertex. Topologically, this forms a **3-ball** (a solid sphere).
* **Link:** The link is the boundary of the star. It consists of the faces of the tetrahedra that are opposite the vertex. Topologically, this forms a **2-sphere** (the surface of a sphere).
