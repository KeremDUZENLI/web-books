### Answer 6.6
**Observation:**
* **Maximum Brightness:** Occurs when $\theta = 0^\circ$ (light vector $l$ aligns with surface normal $n$). $\cos(0)=1$.
* **Diminishing Brightness:** As the object rotates, $\theta$ increases.
* **Black/Dark:** When $\theta \ge 90^\circ$, the surface faces away from the light. $\cos(90)=0$. The term becomes zero (or negative, which is clamped to zero).
