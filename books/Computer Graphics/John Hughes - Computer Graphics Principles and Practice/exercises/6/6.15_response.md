### Answer 6.15
**Solution:**
Create a top-level `Model3DGroup` named "Caravan" and add all the camel instances to it. Apply a `TranslateTransform3D` to this "Caravan" group.

**Scene Graph Structure:**
Caravan (Group) -> Transform (Translation) -> [Camel 1, Camel 2, Camel 3...]

**Loss in Realism:**
Since every camel is a child of the exact same transform group (or identical copies moving in unison), they will move in "lockstep." They will start, stop, and travel at exactly the same speed and position relative to each other, looking like a marching band or robots rather than a natural group of animals.
