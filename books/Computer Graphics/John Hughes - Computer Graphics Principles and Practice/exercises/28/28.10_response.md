### Answer 28.10
    
**Knowns:**
Primaries red ($r$), green ($g$), blue ($b$).
Chromaticity coordinates: $(x_r, y_r), (x_g, y_g), (x_b, y_b)$.
Max luminances: $Y_r, Y_g, Y_b$.

**Derivation:**
1.  Compute the full XYZ triples for the full-brightness primaries:
    $X_r = \frac{x_r}{y_r} Y_r, \quad Z_r = \frac{1-x_r-y_r}{y_r} Y_r$.
    Similarly for $g$ and $b$.
2.  Construct the transformation matrix $M$ using these column vectors:
    $$ M = \begin{bmatrix} X_r & X_g & X_b \\ Y_r & Y_g & Y_b \\ Z_r & Z_g & Z_b \end{bmatrix} $$
3.  Compute XYZ for a color $(r, g, b)$ (where $r,g,b \in [0,1]$ linear intensity):
    $$ \begin{bmatrix} X \\ Y \\ Z \end{bmatrix} = M \begin{bmatrix} r \\ g \\ b \end{bmatrix} $$
    $$ X = r X_r + g X_g + b X_b $$
    $$ Y = r Y_r + g Y_g + b Y_b $$
    $$ Z = r Z_r + g Z_g + b Z_b $$
