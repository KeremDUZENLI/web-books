### Exercise 7.5
The same reasoning as in Exercise 7.4 applies to the implicit form of a line: Depending on the points chosen, we get different "standard implicit forms." Fortunately, all define the same line. Furthermore, any two standard implicit forms are proportional. Write down the implicit form of the line between the points (1, 1) and (2,2). Then find the implicit form of the line between the points (3, 3) and (5,5). Are the two implicit functions identical? Are they proportional? Are the points where they are zero identical?
