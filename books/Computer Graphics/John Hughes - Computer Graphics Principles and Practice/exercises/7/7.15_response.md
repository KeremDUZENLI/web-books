### Answer 7.15

Area($P_0 P_1 P_2$) = Area($Q P_0 P_1$) + Area($Q P_1 P_2$) + Area($Q P_2 P_0$).
Using the origin $Q=(0,0)$, the signed area of triangle $Q P_i P_j$ is $0.5(x_i y_j - x_j y_i)$.
Summing these gives:
$0.5 [ (x_0 y_1 - x_1 y_0) + (x_1 y_2 - x_2 y_1) + (x_2 y_0 - x_0 y_2) ]$.
This matches the standard summation formula.
