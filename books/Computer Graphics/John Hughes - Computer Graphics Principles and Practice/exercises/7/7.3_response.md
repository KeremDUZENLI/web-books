### Answer 7.3

Let $u = [u_x, u_y]^T$ and $v = [v_x, v_y]^T$.
$\times v = [-v_y, v_x]^T$.
$u \cdot (\times v) = u_x(-v_y) + u_y(v_x) = v_x u_y - v_y u_x$.
The determinant of the matrix with columns $v$ and $u$ is:
$det \begin{bmatrix} v_x & u_x \\ v_y & u_y \end{bmatrix} = v_x u_y - v_y u_x$.
The expressions are identical.
