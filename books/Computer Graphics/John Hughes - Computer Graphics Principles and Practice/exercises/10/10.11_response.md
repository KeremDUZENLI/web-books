### Answer 10.11
Relationship: $M_v = C^{-1} M_u C$. Here $C$ is the rotation by $90^\circ$.
**(a)** Rotation matrices commute in 2D. $M_v = R^{-1} M_u R = M_u$. The matrix is identical.
**(b)** Uniform scale matrices ($sI$) commute with all matrices. $M_v = R^{-1} (sI) R = sI = M_u$. The matrix is identical.
**(c)** Nonuniform scale. If $M_u = \text{diag}(a, b)$, then $M_v = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} a & 0 \\ 0 & b \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} b & 0 \\ 0 & a \end{bmatrix}$. The scale factors are swapped.
