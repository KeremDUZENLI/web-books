### Exercise 10.8
Suppose that $T:R^{2}\rightarrow R^{2}$ is a linear transformation represented by the $2\times2$ matrix M, that is, $T(x)=Mx$. Let $K=max_{x\in S^{1}}||T(x)||^{2},$ that is, K is the maximum squared length of all unit vectors transformed by M.
(a) If the SVD of M is $M=UDV^{T}$, show that $K=d_{1}^{2}$.
(b) What is the minimum squared length among all such vectors, in terms of D?
(c) Generalize to $R^{3}$.
