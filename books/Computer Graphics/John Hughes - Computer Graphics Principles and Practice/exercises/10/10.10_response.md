### Answer 10.10
Given matrix $M = \begin{bmatrix} A & t \\ p^T & w \end{bmatrix}$:
**(a)** Affine: $p^T = [0, 0]$ and $w = 1$. (Bottom row is $[0, 0, 1]$).
**(b)** Linear: Affine ($0, 0, 1$ bottom row) and translation $t = [0, 0]^T$.
**(c)** Rotation: Linear ($0, 0, 1$ bottom, $0$ translation) and upper-left $2\times2$ matrix $A$ is orthogonal ($A^T A = I$) with $\det(A) = 1$.
**(d)** Uniform Scale: Linear and $A = sI$ for scalar $s$.
