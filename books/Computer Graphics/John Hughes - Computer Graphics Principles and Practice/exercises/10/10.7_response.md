### Answer 10.7
**(a)** For an outer product $A = uv^T$, every column is a scalar multiple of vector $u$ (column $j$ is $v_j u$). The dimension of the column space is 1, so the rank is 1.
**(b)** The matrix $M_p = \sum_{i=1}^p d_i u_i v_i^T$ is the sum of $p$ orthogonal rank-1 matrices. The vectors $u_1, \dots, u_p$ form an orthogonal basis for the column space. Since there are $p$ basis vectors, the rank is $p$.
