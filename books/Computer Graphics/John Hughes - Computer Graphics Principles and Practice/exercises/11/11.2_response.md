### Answer 11.2
**Matrix:** Diagonal matrix with 1s for the first $k$ entries and -1s for the remaining $n-k$ entries.
$M_{ii} = 1$ for $i \le k$, $M_{ii} = -1$ for $i > k$, $M_{ij} = 0$ for $i \neq j$.
**Orientation:** Determined by the determinant.
$\det(M) = 1^k \cdot (-1)^{n-k} = (-1)^{n-k}$.
If $n-k$ is even, orientation is preserved.
If $n-k$ is odd, orientation is reversed.
