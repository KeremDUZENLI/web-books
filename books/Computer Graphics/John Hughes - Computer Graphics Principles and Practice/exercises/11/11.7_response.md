### Answer 11.7
**$J_{\omega}$:**
$J_{\omega} v = \omega \times v$.
$J_{\omega} \omega = \omega \times \omega = 0$. Eigenvalue $\lambda_1 = 0$, eigenvector $\omega$.
Vectors in the plane perpendicular to $\omega$ are rotated by $90^{\circ}$. No real eigenvectors. Complex eigenvalues $\pm i$.

**$J_{\omega}^2$:**
Eigenvalues are squares of $J_{\omega}$ eigenvalues: $0^2=0$, $i^2=-1$, $(-i)^2=-1$.
$\lambda_1 = 0$, eigenvector $\omega$.
$\lambda_2 = -1$, eigenvectors are any vectors in the plane perpendicular to $\omega$ (plane spanned by $u, v \perp \omega$).
