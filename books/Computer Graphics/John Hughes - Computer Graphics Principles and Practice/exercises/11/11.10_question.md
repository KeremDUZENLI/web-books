### Answer 11.10
**(a) Center B:**
Vectors relative to center: $u = P - B$, $v = Q - B$.
$\theta = \cos^{-1}\left(\frac{u \cdot v}{||u|| ||v||}\right)$.

**(b) Non-unit Sphere:**
Same formula. The denominator $||u|| ||v||$ normalizes the vectors to unit length, effectively computing the angle on the unit sphere.
