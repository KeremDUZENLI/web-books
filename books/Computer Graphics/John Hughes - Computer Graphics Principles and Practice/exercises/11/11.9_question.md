### Exercise 11.9
The skew-symmetric matrix $J_{\omega}$ associated to a vector w is the matrix for the linear transformation $v\mapsto\omega\times v$
(a) Show that every $3\times3$ skew-symmetric matrix S represents the cross product with some vector $\omega$, that is, describe an inverse to the mapping $\omega \rightarrow J_{\omega}$.
(b) Now use this to explain why every $3\times3$ skew-symmetric matrix has O as an eigenvalue, hence det $S=0$.
