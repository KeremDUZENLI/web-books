### Answer 11.3
**Rotation M ($90^{\circ}$ xy-plane / z-axis):**
$M = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$

**Rotation K ($90^{\circ}$ yz-plane / x-axis):**
$K = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$

**Product MK:**
$MK = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$

**Product KM:**
$KM = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$

$MK \neq KM$.
