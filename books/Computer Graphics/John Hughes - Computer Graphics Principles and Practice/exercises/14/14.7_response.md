### Answer 14.7
**Physics:**
* **Green Bottle:** Material absorbs non-green wavelengths. Reflects/transmits green.
* **Red Light:** Emits only red wavelengths. Contains zero green energy.
* **White Table:** Reflects all wavelengths.

**Observations:**
1.  **Bottle:**
    * Incident light: Red.
    * Reflectance: Green (absorbs Red).
    * Result: Absorb Red. No light reflected.
    * **Appearance:** Black.
2.  **Table in Shadow:**
    * Blocked from light by bottle.
    * **Appearance:** Black (Shadow).
3.  **Table out of Shadow:**
    * Incident light: Red.
    * Reflectance: White (reflects Red).
    * **Appearance:** Red.
