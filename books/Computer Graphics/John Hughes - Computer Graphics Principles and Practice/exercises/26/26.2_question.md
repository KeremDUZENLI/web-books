### Exercise 26.2
    
We computed the solid angle subtended by a spherical cap of radius $r<\pi$ on the unit sphere as $2\pi(1-cos(r))$.
(a) What is the solid angle subtended by a spherical cap of radius r on a sphere of radius R?
(b) What is the actual area of a spherical cap of radius r on a sphere of radius R?
