### Answer 8.7

**Implementation Logic:**
1.  **Data Structure:** Maintain a list of vertices `List<Point> vertices`.
2.  **Mouse Click Event:**
    * Get click coordinates $(x, y)$.
    * Add new `Point(x, y)` to `vertices`.
    * Trigger `Invalidate/Redraw`.
3.  **Rendering:**
    * Draw lines connecting $vertices[i]$ to $vertices[i+1]$.
    * Draw small circles at each vertex location.
4.  **Vertex Highlighting:**
    * In the click handler, iterate through `vertices`.
    * Compute distance $d = \sqrt{(v.x - x)^2 + (v.y - y)^2}$.
    * Find min $d$. If $d < threshold$, highlight that vertex (e.g., draw it Red instead of Black).
