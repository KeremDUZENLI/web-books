### Exercise 23.1
    
A bilinear patch is the doubly ruled surface passing through four points $P_{00}, P_{01}, P_{10}$, and $P_{11}$. It is defined by
$$ S(u,v) = (1-u)(1-v)P_{00} + u(1-v)P_{10} + (1-u)vP_{01} + uvP_{11} $$
Show that the trace of the surface in the plane $ax+by+cz=d$ is a conic section. Under what conditions is it a line segment?
