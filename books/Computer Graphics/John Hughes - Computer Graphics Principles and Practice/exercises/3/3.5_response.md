### Answer 3.5

**Direction Vector Effects:**
* **$(0, -1, 0)$:** Light comes from directly above (pointing down the Y-axis). The top of the sphere is brightest; the bottom is dark.
* **$(-1, -1, -1)$:** Light comes from the top-right-front corner. The illumination highlights the upper-right-front quadrant of the sphere.
* **$(0, 0, 1)$:** Light travels in the +Z direction (from behind the object toward the camera). The *back* of the sphere is illuminated. The side facing the camera (Eye at +Z) is in shadow (black), appearing as a silhouette.
