### Exercise 3.8

Framework-3 provides methods for handling keyboard input.

Override the `OnKeyDown` method in your scene class.

1.  Check if the 'W' key is pressed.
2.  If yes, modify the `Camera.Position` by adding to its Z component (or subtracting, depending on your coordinate system) to move the camera forward.
3.  Implement 'S' to move backward.
