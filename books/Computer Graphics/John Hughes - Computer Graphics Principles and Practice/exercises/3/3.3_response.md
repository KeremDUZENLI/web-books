### Answer 3.3

**Observation:**
The sphere looks like a flat circle of uniform gray color.

**Reasoning:**
* **Ambient Light:** Provides constant illumination $I_a$ regardless of position or orientation.
* **Lighting Equation:** $I = I_a \cdot k_a$.
* **Lack of Depth:** Depth perception in 3D rendering relies on shading (gradients), which is caused by the angle between the light source and the surface normal ($\mathbf{n} \cdot \mathbf{l}$). Since ambient light has no direction $\mathbf{l}$, the term $\mathbf{n} \cdot \mathbf{l}$ is irrelevant, resulting in a constant color across the entire surface.
