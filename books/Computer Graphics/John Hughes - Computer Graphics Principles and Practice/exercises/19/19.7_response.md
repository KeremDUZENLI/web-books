### Answer 19.7
    
Checkerboard definition: $C(i,j) = 1$ if $i+j$ is even (white), $0$ if odd (black).

**(a) Odd Rows, Odd Columns:**
Let sampled indices be $I = 2i+1$ and $J = 2j+1$ (odd numbers).
Sum: $I + J = (2i+1) + (2j+1) = 2(i+j) + 2$.
This sum is always even.
Result: $C(I,J) = 1$ (White) everywhere.

**(b) Odd Rows, Even Columns:**
Let sampled indices be $I = 2i+1$ and $J = 2j$ (even numbers).
Sum: $I + J = (2i+1) + 2j = 2(i+j) + 1$.
This sum is always odd.
Result: $C(I,J) = 0$ (Black) everywhere.
