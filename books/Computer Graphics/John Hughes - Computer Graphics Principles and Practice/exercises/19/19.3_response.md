### Answer 19.3
    
This question asks for a numerical estimate strategy or result, as the exact integral depends on the specific form of $B$ (e.g., cubic B-spline).

**Difference:**
* $\bar{B}(x,y) = B(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})$: Radial contours are circles.
* $C(x,y) = B(x)B(y)$: Radial contours are "rounded squares" (squarles).

**Numerical Estimate:**
For the standard cubic B-spline $B_3$, the difference is non-zero but generally small.
$$
\text{Error} = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} | B(x)B(y) - B(\sqrt{x^2+y^2}) | \, dx \, dy
$$
Approximation: The "corners" of the separable filter extend further than the circular filter. The volume difference is typically a few percent of the total filter volume.
