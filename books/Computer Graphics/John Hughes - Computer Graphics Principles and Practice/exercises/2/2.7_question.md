### Exercise 2.7

The more complex a template is, the more value its reusability provides. Add some additional visual elements to the clock-hand template (e.g., a thin line bisecting it longitudinally), or give it a more complex shape and watch how its instances automatically adapt to show the template's new definition.

_Hint:_ The `ControlTemplate` will complain if you put more than one primitive inside it, so you'll need to wrap its contents in a `Canvas` element.
