### Answer 2.7

**Mechanism:**

By wrapping multiple primitives in a `Canvas` within the `ControlTemplate`, they act as a single visual unit.

**Example XAML:**

```xml
<ControlTemplate x:Key="ComplexHandTemplate">
    <Canvas>
        <Polygon Points="..." Fill="Navy"/>
        <Line X1="0" Y1="-1" X2="0" Y2="9" Stroke="White" StrokeThickness="0.05"/>
    </Canvas>
</ControlTemplate>

```

All existing instances (Hour/Minute hands) immediately render with the white stripe included.
