### Exercise 13.8
(a) Stand facing a wall, extend your arms horizontally to the sides, and wiggle your fingers. Move forward or backward until you can just detect the motion in your peripheral vision while fixating on a point in front of you. Measure the angle subtended by your arms at your eyes.
(b) Have a friend stand behind you with hands out, showing 1, 2, or 3 fingers. Ask them to move forward until you can identify the number of fingers while fixating forward. Measure the angle subtended by their hands at your eyes.
