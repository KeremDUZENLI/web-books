### Exercise 4.4

Currently, the robot can only move up/down (translation). Add the ability to rotate the entire robot.

1.  Replace the torso's single `TranslateTransform3D` with a `Transform3DGroup`.
2.  Add the existing translation to the group.
3.  Create a new `RotateTransform3D` (using `AxisAngleRotation3D` with axis $(0,1,0)$) and add it to the group.
4.  Bind the rotation angle to a new Tweak Bar slider.
